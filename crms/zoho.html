What is Zoho CRM?

Zoho CRM is part of the Zoho ecosystem—a suite of 50+ business applications. It's known for offering Salesforce-like functionality at a fraction of the cost.

With features spanning sales automation, marketing, customer support, and analytics, Zoho CRM is a versatile choice for businesses wanting comprehensive tools without breaking the bank.

Who is Zoho CRM best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • Budget-conscious teams wanting full features
  • Companies using Zoho suite (Books, Projects, etc.)
  • SMBs needing all-in-one platform
  • Teams willing to invest in setup

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Teams wanting polished UX
  • Quick, simple implementations
  • Companies needing premium support
  • Heavy third-party integration needs
