What is Freshdesk?

Freshdesk is a customer support platform that helps companies manage customer inquiries across email, chat, phone, social media, and web. It's part of the Freshworks family and integrates well with Freshsales for sales+support alignment.

While not a traditional sales CRM, Freshdesk serves as a customer relationship tool for support-focused teams and can be paired with Freshsales for full customer lifecycle management.

Who is Freshdesk best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • Customer support teams
  • Companies needing helpdesk + CRM
  • Omnichannel support needs
  • Teams wanting free tier to start

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Pure sales teams
  • Teams needing sales pipelines
  • Lead generation focused
  • Simple use cases (can be overkill)
