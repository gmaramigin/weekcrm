What is Folk?

Folk is a CRM that takes a relationship-first approach rather than a deal-first approach. It's designed for people who manage contacts across various contexts—investors tracking founders, agencies managing clients, recruiters tracking candidates.

The interface feels more like Notion than Salesforce, making it approachable for non-salespeople who still need to manage relationships professionally.

Who is Folk best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • VC/PE firms tracking founders and deals
  • Agencies managing client relationships
  • Recruiters and talent teams
  • Teams wanting flexibility over rigid pipelines

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Traditional B2B sales teams
  • Teams needing complex reporting
  • Heavy automation requirements
  • Enterprise-scale deployments
