What is Pipedrive?

Pipedrive is a sales CRM designed to help sales teams track deals and activities. Its visual pipeline interface lets you see exactly where every deal stands at a glance.

Founded in 2010, Pipedrive has grown to serve over 100,000 companies. The philosophy is simple: reduce busywork so salespeople can focus on selling.

Who is Pipedrive best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • Sales-focused teams who live in their pipeline
  • SMBs wanting simplicity over complexity
  • Teams new to CRM with minimal training time
  • Deal-driven sales with clear stages

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Marketing-heavy teams needing automation
  • Customer support workflows
  • Complex B2B enterprise sales
  • Teams needing a free plan
