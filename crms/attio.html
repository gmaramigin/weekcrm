What is Attio?

Attio is a next-generation CRM that combines the flexibility of a database with the power of a CRM. It automatically captures and enriches contact data, syncing with your email and calendar.

What sets Attio apart is its data model—you can create custom objects and relationships, making it adaptable to virtually any workflow or use case.
