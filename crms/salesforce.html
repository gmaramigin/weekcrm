What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is the world's leading CRM platform, used by over 150,000 companies globally. Founded in 1999, it pioneered cloud-based CRM software and has grown into a comprehensive business platform.

The platform includes Sales Cloud (CRM), Service Cloud (support), Marketing Cloud, Commerce Cloud, and more. It's known for extreme customizability—you can build almost anything on the platform.

However, this power comes at a cost: Salesforce is complex, expensive, and typically requires trained administrators or consultants to set up and maintain properly.

Who is Salesforce best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • Large enterprises with complex sales processes
  • Companies needing deep customization
  • Organizations with dedicated IT/admin resources
  • Multi-department deployments
  • Companies with budget for proper implementation

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Small businesses under 20 people
  • Budget-conscious teams
  • Simple sales processes
  • Teams without technical resources
  • Quick implementations needed

Our Verdict

Salesforce is an excellent choice if: You're a mid-size to enterprise company with complex needs, have budget for proper implementation, and have (or will hire) technical resources to manage it.

Look elsewhere if: You're a small team, need something quick to set up, or don't have budget for both licenses AND implementation/admin costs.

Salesforce can do almost anything, but the question is whether you need that power and can afford the total cost of ownership.
