What is Breakcold?

Breakcold is a CRM built specifically for social selling. It pulls in LinkedIn and Twitter posts from your prospects, letting you engage with their content before sending a cold email.

The idea is to "warm up" prospects by commenting on their posts and building familiarity before pitching—making outreach feel less cold.

Who is Breakcold best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • B2B sales teams using LinkedIn heavily
  • Social sellers and personal brands
  • Agency owners and consultants
  • Founders doing outbound

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Traditional sales pipelines
  • Non-social selling workflows
  • Large enterprise teams
  • Teams not active on LinkedIn/X
