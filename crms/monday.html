What is Monday Sales CRM?

Monday Sales CRM is a dedicated sales product built on Monday.com's Work OS platform. It takes the flexibility that made Monday famous and applies it specifically to sales workflows.

The result is a highly visual, customizable CRM that feels more like building blocks than a rigid system. Teams can create exactly the workflow they need.

Who is Monday Sales CRM best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • Teams wanting flexibility to build their own workflows
  • Companies already using Monday
  • Hybrid sales + project teams
  • Visual thinkers who love Kanban views

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Solo salespeople (3-seat minimum)
  • Teams needing built-in calling
  • Budget-constrained large teams
  • Traditional sales workflows
