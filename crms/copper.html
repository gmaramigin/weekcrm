What is Copper?

Copper (formerly ProsperWorks) is the only CRM recommended by Google. It's designed to work seamlessly within Google Workspace, appearing as a sidebar in Gmail.

The key benefit is zero data entry—Copper automatically captures contacts, tracks emails, and logs meetings from Google Calendar.

Who is Copper best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • Google Workspace teams
  • Relationship-based sales (consulting, agencies)
  • Teams wanting minimal data entry
  • Small to mid-size teams

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Microsoft 365 users
  • Teams needing advanced reporting
  • Complex enterprise sales
  • Heavy automation needs
