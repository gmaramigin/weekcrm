What is Freshsales?

Freshsales is a sales CRM from Freshworks that combines contact management with built-in phone, email, and chat. The idea is to give salespeople everything they need in one tool.

The standout feature is Freddy AI, which provides lead scoring, deal insights, and suggested next actions to help prioritize sales efforts.

Who is Freshsales best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • Teams wanting built-in calling
  • SMBs with straightforward sales processes
  • Freshworks users (Freshdesk, Freshservice)
  • Teams valuing clean UX

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Heavy integration needs
  • Complex enterprise sales
  • Teams needing deep customization
  • Marketing-focused teams
