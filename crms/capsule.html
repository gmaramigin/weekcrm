What is Capsule CRM?

Capsule CRM is a simple, elegant CRM that's been serving small businesses since 2009. It focuses on contact management, sales pipeline tracking, and task organization.

The philosophy is simplicity over features. Capsule won't overwhelm you with options—it just helps you keep track of relationships and follow up consistently.

Who is Capsule CRM best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • Small businesses wanting simplicity
  • First-time CRM users
  • Service businesses (consultants, agencies)
  • Teams who hate complex software

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Teams needing advanced automation
  • Marketing-heavy organizations
  • Complex enterprise sales
  • Large sales teams
