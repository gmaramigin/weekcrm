What is Close?

Close is a CRM designed for inside sales teams who spend their days making calls and sending emails. Everything is built for speed—minimal clicks, fast search, and communication tools right in the CRM.

The power dialer feature is particularly notable—it can automatically dial through a list of leads, making high-volume outreach much more efficient.

Who is Close best for?

✓ Great fit for:

  • Inside sales teams doing high-volume outreach
  • SDR/BDR teams focused on prospecting
  • Teams wanting all-in-one calls + email + CRM
  • Fast-paced sales environments

✗ Not ideal for:

  • Complex enterprise sales with long cycles
  • Field sales teams
  • Teams needing deep customization
  • Marketing + sales alignment needs
