Breakcold

Breakcold is a cold outreach CRM for solopreneurs and small teams. It merges email, LinkedIn, and pipeline tracking into a single tool designed for building warm relationships at scale.

Go to site

What’s that?

Breakcold is a lightweight sales CRM that prioritizes outbound communication. Unlike traditional CRMs, it combines deal tracking with social selling — letting you see what leads post on LinkedIn, send outreach emails, and manage deals from one screen.

For whom?

Perfect for solo founders, cold email specialists, LinkedIn sellers, and early-stage startup teams that rely on outbound to grow. If you want speed and clarity over complexity, this tool fits.

Strengths

  • Unified inbox for LinkedIn, email, and Twitter DMs
  • Daily social feed from leads to start warm conversations
  • Custom pipelines and stage-based filtering
  • Email outreach with open/reply tracking
  • Chrome extension for LinkedIn prospecting

What’s worth considering?

  • No built-in calling or meeting scheduling
  • Limited integrations with larger CRMs
  • Designed more for solo and small teams than large orgs

Conclusion

Breakcold feels like a modern CRM stripped down for fast-paced outbound. If your sales strategy is “email + LinkedIn + hustle,” this tool gives you everything without the bloat. Especially valuable for 1–5 person teams doing cold outreach at scale.
