Close CRM

Close is the CRM purpose-built for outbound sales. Built-in calling, email, and automation help reps close deals faster — without switching tabs.

Go to site

What’s that?

Close CRM is an all-in-one sales platform built to accelerate outbound workflows. It combines calling, emailing, lead management, and reporting in a single, easy-to-use interface.

For whom?

Perfect for fast-moving sales teams, especially in SaaS, agencies, and startups. If your team works the phones and inboxes all day, Close keeps everything in one place.

Strengths

  • Built-in Power Dialer and call tracking
  • Email sequences, templates, and open/click tracking
  • One-click calling and call recording included
  • Smart Views for automated follow-ups
  • API access for integrations and custom workflows

What’s worth considering?

  • Geared mostly toward outbound; not ideal for complex B2B opportunity flows
  • No freemium plan — pricing starts at $49/month
  • Customization limited compared to platforms like Salesforce or Attio

Conclusion

If your team does high-volume outreach and needs speed, Close delivers. It's one of the few CRMs with true built-in calling and emailing — no extra tools required.
