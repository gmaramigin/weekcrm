Pricing & Costs

CRM pricing varies based on features, user limits, storage, and support levels. Enterprise CRMs like Salesforce include deep customization and extensive support, while simpler tools focus on core functionality at lower prices. Watch out for per-user vs. per-seat vs. per-contact pricing models—they add up differently.

Common hidden costs include: implementation/onboarding fees, per-contact charges (especially for marketing CRMs), storage overages, premium support, add-on features like phone calling or advanced automation, and annual contract requirements for best pricing. We highlight these in our pricing breakdowns.

Annual plans typically save 15-30% compared to monthly billing. However, we recommend starting monthly while you're testing a CRM to avoid being locked into something that doesn't fit. Once you're confident, switch to annual to save money.
