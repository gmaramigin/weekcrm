Vendasta has introduced two new features to Vibe, its AI-powered business tool: Sessions and an Ask mode. Sessions let users create dedicated, topic-specific conversations with the AI, keeping context isolated so that work on multiple objectives does not become cluttered. Previous session history remains accessible through an "all messages" area beneath the session selector.

The new Ask mode provides an interactive, back-and-forth discussion format designed for brainstorming. Context built during an Ask conversation automatically carries forward when users switch to Vibe's plan or build modes, removing the need to re-explain objectives. The features are available now to users who activate Vibe in the Vendasta marketplace and access it through Business App under AI > Vibe in the left sidebar.
