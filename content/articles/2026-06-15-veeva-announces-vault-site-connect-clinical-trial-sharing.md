Veeva Systems has announced Vault Site Connect, a new application designed to automate information sharing between sponsors and clinical trial sites. The tool aims to streamline the exchange of documents and data during clinical trials, reducing manual processes that typically slow down site-sponsor communication. Vault Site Connect is built on Veeva's Vault platform and targets life sciences organizations looking to improve efficiency in their clinical operations workflows. Further details on pricing and availability were not disclosed in the initial announcement.
