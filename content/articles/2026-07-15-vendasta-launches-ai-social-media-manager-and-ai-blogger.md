Vendasta has introduced two AI-driven tools within its Social AI Premium tier: an AI Social Media Manager and an AI Blogger, both designed to automate weekly content production for local businesses and channel partners.

The AI Social Media Manager generates and publishes social posts across Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, X, and other platforms based on a business's goals and brand voice. It pairs posts with AI-generated or stock visuals and provides performance insights. The AI Blogger produces one FAQ-style, SEO-optimized blog post per week, publishing directly to WordPress or saving drafts for review.

Alongside the launch, Vendasta has rebranded its Social Marketing product line as Social AI, now offered in three marketplace editions: Standard, Professional, and Premium. A white-glove setup service is available for partners who want expert configuration.
