Method CRM has released a pair of bug fixes now live for all users. The first resolves an issue where the currency field was not visible before syncing on new or edited Estimate and Bill screens. The second addresses a problem where signing in with Microsoft could fail and display an unexpected error page after authentication. Both fixes are identified by internal tracking numbers PL-61677 and PL-61842, respectively. The updates were published on April 24, 2026.
