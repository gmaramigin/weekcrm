Method CRM released a bug fix addressing an issue in its editable grid where sales receipts sharing the same date were sorted by a hidden time-of-day value rather than consistently by record ID. The update, which went live on June 23, 2026, ensures that same-date sales receipts now display in a predictable order based on record ID. The fix is tracked under ticket PL-63721.
