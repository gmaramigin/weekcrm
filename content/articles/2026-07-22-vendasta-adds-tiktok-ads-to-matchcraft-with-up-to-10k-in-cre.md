Vendasta has launched TikTok advertising within its MatchCraft platform, allowing channel partners to offer managed TikTok campaigns to local business clients. Vendasta's ad team handles campaign setup, creative production, targeting, and optimization, while reporting and budget controls appear in the existing MatchCraft dashboard alongside other channels.

Vendasta has also been designated a TikTok Channel Sales Partner, a program limited to a small group of ad tech platforms recognized by TikTok for delivering advertising at scale.

Eligible advertisers—businesses with zero prior TikTok ad spend that commit at least $250 per month—can receive tier-based matched ad credits ranging up to $10,000 during a 60-day qualification window. Credits expire at the end of the quarter following registration. The offer runs through December 31, 2026.
