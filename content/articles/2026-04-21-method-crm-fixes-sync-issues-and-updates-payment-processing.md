Method CRM released a set of updates on April 20, 2026, addressing several bugs and refining its payment workflow. The Process Payment Action now triggers its selected App Routine when a payment status is Pending rather than at a later stage. On the fixes side, a QuickBooks Desktop sync issue that caused deleted Price Levels to persist in Method has been resolved. The update also addresses performance lag when switching apps and opening records, eliminates redundant data reloads on legacy grids when clicking a record, and restores availability of certain session variables in editable grids. All changes are live for current Method users.
