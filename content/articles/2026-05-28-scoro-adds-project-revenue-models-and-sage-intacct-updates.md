Scoro has released its June 2026 version update, introducing new project revenue models designed to align revenue tracking with how contracts are actually structured. The update allows teams to match revenue recognition to specific contract types, aiming to improve financial precision. The release also includes updates to the platform's Sage Intacct integration, though specific details on the scope of those changes were not disclosed. The new features are available now to Scoro users.
