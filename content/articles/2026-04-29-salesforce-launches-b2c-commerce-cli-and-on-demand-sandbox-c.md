Salesforce has introduced a unified command-line interface (CLI) for B2C Commerce along with an on-demand sandbox cloning capability, both aimed at accelerating development workflows. The release also includes AI-powered agent skills for B2C Commerce developers. The new CLI consolidates development tooling into a single interface, while on-demand sandbox cloning allows developers to spin up environment copies as needed. Further details are available on the Salesforce Developers Blog.
