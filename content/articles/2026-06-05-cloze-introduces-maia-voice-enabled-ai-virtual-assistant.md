Cloze has introduced Maia, a voice-enabled AI virtual assistant designed for real estate professionals. The assistant allows agents to interact with their CRM using voice commands, enabling hands-free access to contact details, transaction updates, and task management. Maia leverages the relationship data already stored in Cloze's platform to provide contextual responses and support agents on the go. The launch targets busy real estate professionals who need quick access to client information without manually navigating their CRM. Further details on pricing and availability were not disclosed in the announcement.
