HoneyBook has expanded its clientflow management platform to the United Kingdom and Australia, marking the company's first availability outside the United States. The launch opens the platform to independent small business owners and service providers in both countries. HoneyBook, which offers tools for managing client bookings, invoicing, contracts, and payments, says it already serves hundreds of thousands of independent service providers. Specific pricing or feature differences for the new markets were not disclosed.
