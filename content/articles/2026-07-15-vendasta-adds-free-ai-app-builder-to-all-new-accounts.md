Vendasta will automatically include Vibe (Beta) | Free in the default product set for every new account created through Partner Center starting August 3, 2026. The addition comes at no extra cost to partners or their clients. Vibe lets end users build AI-driven applications and websites without technical setup, and each client account receives 250 daily usage credits. Partners who want early access can enable the feature now via Partner Center > Marketplace > Products. Those who prefer to opt out can do so before the August 3 deadline; existing accounts will not be affected unless partners explicitly request activation. The move is aimed at reducing onboarding friction and giving new clients immediate access to AI tooling.
