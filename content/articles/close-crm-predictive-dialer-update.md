Close has rolled out AI call scoring as part of its Predictive Dialer feature, available on Business and Enterprise plans.

## What's new

Every outbound call made through Close's dialer is now automatically scored by AI on key metrics:

- **Talk-to-listen ratio** — how much the rep spoke vs. listened
- **Objection handling** — whether common objections were addressed
- **Next steps** — did the call end with a clear follow-up?

Scores appear on the call record and can be filtered in reporting. Managers can flag low-scoring calls for coaching review.

## Why it matters

Call coaching has traditionally required managers to listen to recordings manually — a process that doesn't scale. With automatic scoring, teams can focus review time on the calls that need attention most.

For sales teams doing 50+ calls per day, this is a meaningful time saver.

## Availability

AI call scoring is included at no extra cost for Business and Enterprise plan customers. Startup plan users do not have access.
