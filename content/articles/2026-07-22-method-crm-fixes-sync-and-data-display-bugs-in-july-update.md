Method CRM released a series of bug fixes on July 15, 2026, addressing issues across several areas of its platform. The update resolves a sync problem where assigned price levels did not fully transfer from QuickBooks Desktop. Sales order line items from deleted or archived orders no longer appear in grids and reports. A bug causing refund receipts to display outdated data after renaming a custom sales field has been corrected. Additionally, multi-select field values that could appear blank in grids and exports now display properly. The fixes are live for all Method users.
