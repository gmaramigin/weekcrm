Attio just shipped a notable quality-of-life improvement: meeting management is now accessible directly from Quick Actions.

## What changed

The Quick Actions panel — Attio's centralized command bar — now includes a **Meeting Overview** section. From here you can:

- See today's and tomorrow's upcoming meetings at a glance
- Prepare for calls by reviewing related contact and company records
- Start, stop, or control meeting recordings without leaving the CRM

## Why it matters

Before this update, managing meetings in Attio meant navigating to separate views or relying on your calendar app. Now the workflow is consolidated: open Quick Actions, check your schedule, review context, and jump into the meeting — all from one place.

For sales teams running back-to-back calls, this saves real time. No more tab-switching between calendar, CRM, and meeting tools.

## Who benefits most

Teams already using Attio's recording and note-taking features will get the most out of this. If you're on a plan that supports meeting recordings, this is an immediate workflow improvement.
