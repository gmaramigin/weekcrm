Method CRM released a patch addressing two bugs in its multi-entity functionality. The first fix resolves an issue where records could fail to save due to an invalid or missing TenantID error. The second corrects a sizing problem in the Super Admin invite flow, ensuring the modal now adjusts to its content and scrolls when needed. Both updates are live as of May 27, 2026.
