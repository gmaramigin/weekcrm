Cloze has been selected as the AI foundation for Raveis365+, a new technology stack rolled out by William Raveis Real Estate to its approximately 4,500 agents. The deployment, reported by HousingWire, integrates Cloze AI into the brokerage's operations to support its agent workforce. William Raveis is one of the largest independent real estate brokerages in the United States. Specific details about the Raveis365+ platform's capabilities and rollout timeline were not disclosed in the available source material.
