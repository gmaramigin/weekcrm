OpenCRM has updated its Clickthrough Wizard, the tool used to create, track, and manage interactions from email campaigns. The revised interface lets users monitor engagement metrics including clicks, opens, subscribes, and unsubscribes, with the ability to drill down into individual recipient activity. The update is aimed at marketers who need both high-level campaign performance views and detailed user-behavior analysis.
