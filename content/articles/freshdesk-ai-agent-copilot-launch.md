Freshdesk has launched Freddy AI Agent Copilot, an AI assistant that works alongside support agents in real time.

## What it does

The copilot sits in the ticket sidebar and provides:

- **Response suggestions** — drafts a reply based on ticket context and knowledge base articles
- **Ticket summaries** — condenses long threads into a brief overview for handoffs
- **Smart routing** — recommends which team or specialist should handle escalated tickets
- **Sentiment detection** — flags frustrated customers for priority handling

## Why it matters

Support teams handling high ticket volumes spend significant time on repetitive responses and context-switching between tickets. The copilot reduces both — agents get a draft they can review and send, plus instant context on any ticket they pick up.

For teams doing 100+ tickets/day, the time savings compound quickly.

## Pricing

Freddy AI Copilot is included in the Pro and Enterprise plans at no additional cost. Growth plan users can add it as a paid add-on.
