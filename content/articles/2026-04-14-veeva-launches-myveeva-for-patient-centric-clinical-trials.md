Veeva Systems announced MyVeeva, a platform designed to streamline patient engagement in clinical trials. The offering aims to give trial participants a unified digital experience, consolidating activities such as eConsent, telehealth visits, and data collection into a single application. MyVeeva is built on Veeva's Vault platform and is intended for sponsors and clinical research organizations seeking to reduce patient burden and improve retention in studies. The app provides patients with a consistent interface across trials and sites, eliminating the need to download separate tools for each study. By centralizing patient-facing interactions, Veeva positions MyVeeva as an alternative to the fragmented mix of point solutions commonly used in clinical operations today. The company has not disclosed specific pricing or a general availability date.
