Method CRM has released a patch addressing a navigation issue within its app ribbon interface. The fix resolves a bug where the "Back" and "Save & Back" buttons inside an app ribbon failed to return users to the previously opened screen. The update, tracked as PL-62012, is now live for all Method users.
