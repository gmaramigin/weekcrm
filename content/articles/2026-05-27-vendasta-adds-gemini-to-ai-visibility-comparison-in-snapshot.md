Vendasta has added Google Gemini as a third AI model in the AI Optimization (AEO) section of its Snapshot Report, joining Google AI Mode and ChatGPT. The update gives partners a three-way comparison of how a business appears across AI-generated search responses, using identical personas and prompts for each model to ensure consistent results.

The feature is designed to help agencies and channel partners identify model-specific gaps in AI search visibility — for example, a business that surfaces in ChatGPT but not Gemini — and use those findings to strengthen pitches for AI Optimization and SEO services. Gemini data appears automatically in Snapshot Reports created after 1 May 2026 when the AI Optimization section is enabled. The AEO section continues to support translated prompts for non-English regions. Older reports retain their original model coverage.
