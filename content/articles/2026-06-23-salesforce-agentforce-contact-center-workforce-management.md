Salesforce Service Cloud has introduced Agentforce Contact Center Workforce Engagement Management, a tool designed to help contact center supervisors optimize staffing capacity, develop service rep skills, and gain visibility into customer interactions. The offering unifies workforce management across both AI agents and human service representatives, allowing supervisors to manage blended workforces from a single platform. The product aims to address the operational complexity that arises as organizations deploy AI agents alongside human reps in their contact centers, providing coordinated scheduling, coaching, and performance monitoring capabilities.
