Method CRM released a batch of enhancements and bug fixes across its platform on June 2, 2026. The update adds clear UI messaging during password reset and change-password flows when a password cannot be reused. The Payments and Cases list screens now feature editable grids and refreshed button styling. On the fixes side, Method resolved an issue where payment gateway options remained visible on estimates after a deposit was collected, corrected a filtering error in estimate deposit preferences, and patched a bug that allowed recurring donation plans to revert to inactive saved payment methods. Additional fixes address failed URL redirects after Web To Lead form submissions and broken date-field comparison logic in the EmailsSent table for Actions criteria.
