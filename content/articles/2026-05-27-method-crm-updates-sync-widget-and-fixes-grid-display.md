Method CRM released updates on May 26, 2026, introducing an enhancement to its sync widget and fixing an editable grid issue. The sync widget is now available as a control within the show/hide action, giving app builders more flexibility in managing widget visibility. The release also addresses a bug where column visibility in the editable grid did not preview correctly in the app builder's mobile viewport. Both changes are live for current Method users.
