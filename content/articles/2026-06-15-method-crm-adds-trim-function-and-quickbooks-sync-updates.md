Method CRM released a set of updates on June 10, 2026, introducing a Trim function within its Text Functions actions. The new capability lets users remove leading and trailing whitespace, collapse internal spaces, preserve newlines, and treat tabs as spaces. The release also adds bidirectional sync support for the Ship From Address field on Invoice, Estimate, and Sales Receipt transactions in QuickBooks Online. On the bug-fix side, Method resolved an issue where a full-width Attachments widget on a custom screen could push the App Ribbon off-screen. A separate fix addresses Vendor Bill creation from Purchase Orders on accounts using auto-assigned reference numbers, replacing a cryptic error with a save warning that identifies the problematic value.
