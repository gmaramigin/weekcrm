OpenCRM now allows users to include Contacts as a secondary module within Asset reports. The update enables reporting on assets alongside their associated primary contact details.

When Contacts are added as a secondary module, an "Email Contacts" button appears, allowing users to send bulk communications directly from report results. The change benefits teams that manage assets tied to specific contacts and need to combine asset reporting with outreach workflows.
