Folk has upgraded its LinkedIn integration to pull significantly more data when enriching contacts.

## What changed

When you add a contact from LinkedIn (via browser extension or manual import), Folk now captures:

- Current and previous job titles with dates
- Mutual connections count
- Recent LinkedIn activity summary
- Company size and industry from the company page

Previously, enrichment was limited to name, title, company, and email. The expanded data populates automatically into custom fields.

## Practical impact

For recruiting teams and partnership managers, having job history and activity context directly in the CRM eliminates the back-and-forth between LinkedIn and your contact database.

The mutual connections data is particularly useful for warm introductions — you can quickly see who on your team is already connected to a prospect.

## Availability

Available on all paid Folk plans. Free plan users get limited enrichment (name, title, company only).
