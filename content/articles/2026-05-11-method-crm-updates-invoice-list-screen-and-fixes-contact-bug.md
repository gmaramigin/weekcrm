Method CRM has released a set of updates as of May 7, 2026. The invoice list screen now features a new grid component and updated button styling. The release also addresses two bugs: a fix for the Customers & Leads module where the Contact Type selection did not persist when users navigated from the Quick-Add Modal to the full New Contact form, and a multi-entity fix that restored missing app access for Super Admins.
