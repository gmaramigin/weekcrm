Veeva Systems and IQVIA have announced long-term clinical and commercial partnerships along with the resolution of all outstanding disputes between the two companies. The agreements cover both clinical and commercial domains, signaling a shift from legal conflict to strategic collaboration between two major life sciences technology providers. Specific financial terms and detailed partnership scopes were not disclosed in the announcement.
