Vendasta has released Vibe, a prompt-based builder now in beta that lets users create websites, apps, and dashboards by describing what they need in natural language. Vibe automatically pulls existing business details, connects forms directly to Vendasta's CRM, and handles hosting, security, and deployment so projects can go live immediately after publishing.

The tool targets channel partners, franchise and multi-location teams, and SMB users. Vendasta offers three tiers: a Free plan with 250 daily credits, one project, and subdomain hosting; a Standard plan with 10,000 monthly credits, up to five projects, custom domains, analytics, SSO, and white-labeling; and a Pro plan with 25,000 monthly credits, unlimited projects, downloadable project files, and a Supabase external connector. Add-on credit packs are available in increments of 10,000 to 100,000.
