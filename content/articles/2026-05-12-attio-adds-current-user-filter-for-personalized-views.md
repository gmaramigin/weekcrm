Attio now lets workspace members filter any user-type attribute by "Current user," so that views, reports, and lists automatically adjust to whoever is logged in. The filter can be applied to any attribute that stores a person, including record owner, assigned rep, and created-by fields. Sales reps, for example, can open a shared pipeline view and see only their own deals without building a separate filter. The capability works across views, reports, and list filters, eliminating the need for individually configured saved views per team member.
