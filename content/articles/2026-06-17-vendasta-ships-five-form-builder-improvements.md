Vendasta has released five updates to its Forms tool aimed at reducing friction for partners and SMBs managing forms at scale. Users can now duplicate any existing form with a single click and preview the copy before confirming. A revised embed script allows multiple distinct forms on a single page with isolated CSS, eliminating the previous iframe workaround. Post-submission behavior is now customizable, with options for a branded thank-you page, a redirect URL, or a resubmit button. Two internationalization upgrades round out the release: validation error messages such as "invalid email" now display in the visitor's language based on geolocation, and form-submission notification emails automatically follow the recipient's language rather than the language in which the form was built. Automations are not copied during form duplication.
