Method CRM released a batch of bug fixes on July 7, 2026. The update resolves an issue where copying actions into an App Routine failed when the source included a Show Toast Notification action. A separate fix addresses proposal acceptance failures in the portal caused by a push notification error.

The Tags dropdown now loads faster for accounts with large tag lists. Additionally, percentage-based per-item price levels synced from QuickBooks Desktop are now correctly applied to sales order line items. All fixes are live for current Method users.
