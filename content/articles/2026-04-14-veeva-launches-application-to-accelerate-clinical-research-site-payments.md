Veeva Systems has introduced a new application within its Vault platform designed to speed up payments to clinical research sites. The tool aims to address persistent delays in site payments, which have long been a pain point in clinical trial operations. By automating payment workflows and providing greater visibility into payment status, the application is intended to reduce administrative burden for both sponsors and research sites. Faster, more predictable payments can help improve site satisfaction and retention, which are critical factors in trial enrollment and execution. The new offering expands Veeva's Vault Clinical Operations suite, which already includes tools for study startup, site management, and clinical data management. Specific pricing and availability details were not disclosed in the announcement.
