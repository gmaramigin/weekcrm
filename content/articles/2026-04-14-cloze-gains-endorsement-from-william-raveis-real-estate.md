Cloze, the AI-powered CRM platform for real estate professionals, was introduced by Ryan Raveis at the William Raveis Real Estate Elite Event. Raveis, who leads one of the largest family-owned real estate companies in the Northeast, presented Cloze to the firm's top-performing agents as a recommended relationship management tool. The endorsement signals Cloze's growing traction among high-volume real estate teams seeking AI-driven contact and deal management. William Raveis Real Estate operates across multiple states with thousands of agents, making the introduction a significant distribution opportunity for Cloze within the residential brokerage segment. Details on whether the rollout involves a formal enterprise agreement or remains an optional agent-level adoption were not disclosed.
