Unthread released a batch of product updates for May 2026, including improvements to its MCP (Model Context Protocol) integration, a new channel health scores feature, and scheduled reports. Channel health scores give support teams visibility into the performance and responsiveness of individual conversation channels. Scheduled reports allow users to automate recurring delivery of key support metrics. The release also includes additional unspecified enhancements. Specific pricing or availability details were not disclosed.
