Method CRM released a set of enhancements and bug fixes on June 9, 2026. The update converts the Email Campaigns and Web To Lead list screens to editable grids with refreshed button styling. On the fixes side, Method resolved an issue where the "View selected records" toggle in editable grids could blank out results, and corrected a mobile app bug that prevented searching transactions by reference number. Additional fixes address multi-select dropdown events not firing, a 401 error blocking customer portal sign-in, recurring proposal invoices being skipped when due on the last day of a contract, and copied closed Purchase Orders retaining a Closed status instead of defaulting to Open.
