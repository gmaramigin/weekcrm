Method CRM has released updates that replace the existing Vendors List with editable grids, adding search, filtering, sorting, and CSV export capabilities. The change is aimed at giving users more flexible ways to manage and navigate vendor data directly within the list view.

The June 24, 2026 release also includes a bug fix for the Account Settings section, where the Communication screen previously displayed a failed retrieval error on billing day.
