Monday CRM has added an AI email composer directly within its deal and contact views.

## How it works

When composing an email from a deal card, the AI composer can:

- Draft a first outreach email based on the deal stage and contact details
- Suggest follow-up messages based on previous email threads
- Adjust tone (formal, casual, brief) with a single click

The feature uses deal context — stage, value, notes — to personalize drafts. You review and edit before sending.

## What's good

The contextual awareness is the differentiator here. Unlike generic AI writing tools, Monday's composer reads your deal data and previous interactions. For teams that already keep detailed deal notes, the drafts are surprisingly relevant.

## What to consider

- Only available on Pro and Enterprise plans
- English-language drafts only at launch
- Quality depends heavily on how well your deal records are maintained

## Bottom line

A useful addition for teams already invested in Monday's ecosystem. Not a reason to switch CRMs, but a solid productivity boost if you're already there.
