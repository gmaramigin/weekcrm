Salesforce has released its April Sales Cloud update, headlined by an AI-driven prospecting agent within the Agentforce Sales suite that is designed to operate around the clock on behalf of sales reps. The release bundles six notable capabilities aimed at helping sales teams automate outreach and pipeline management.

Specific feature details and pricing were not disclosed in the announcement beyond the positioning of the Agentforce-powered prospecting agent as a 24/7 assistant. The update is available now to Sales Cloud customers.
