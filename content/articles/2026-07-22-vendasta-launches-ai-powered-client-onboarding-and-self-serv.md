Vendasta is rolling out a redesigned onboarding experience that automatically activates new client accounts using its AI Workforce. Starting with Early Access on July 23, every new account created will trigger automated business research, a live website build, an AI receptionist trained on the business, and a visibility scan — all before the client logs in.

The updated onboarding email replaces a standard password-setup prompt with a summary of completed work, including the generated website, directory scans, and a pre-filled business profile. When the Vibe feature is enabled, sending the email automatically creates the demo website. Clients then log into Business App to see finished deliverables rather than an empty dashboard.

Vendasta is also introducing self-serve upgrades within Business App. Clients can purchase additional products directly — paying by card through Vendasta Payments — or submit an order for the partner to close manually. Partners control pricing, product selection, and whether the self-serve experience is enabled at all.
