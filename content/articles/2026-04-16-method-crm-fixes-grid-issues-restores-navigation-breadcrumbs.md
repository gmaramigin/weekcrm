Method CRM released a set of bug fixes and a navigation improvement on April 15, 2026. The update addresses two grid-related issues: one where selectable rows appeared frozen on save, and another where grids displayed unexpected loading behavior on click before navigation. Additionally, Method restored breadcrumbs to the multi-entity management page within Account Settings, providing clearer navigation for users managing multiple entities. All updates are now live.
