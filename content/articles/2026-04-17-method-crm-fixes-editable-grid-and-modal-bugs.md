Method CRM released a pair of bug fixes on April 17, 2026. The first resolves an issue in editable grids where currency-formatted fields disappeared after an update. The second addresses a problem where modals and show-message dialogs persisted when users clicked the browser's back or forward buttons. Both fixes are now live for all Method users.
