## About Good Steward Network

Good Steward Network (GSN) is a business operations agency focused on small and mid-sized organizations that have outgrown spreadsheets but do not have a RevOps team. They treat Attio as the hub of a simplified tech stack — not a monster with every possible integration turned on.

## What they do

- **Opinionated Attio builds** for teams that want "the right amount" of CRM, not everything at once.
- **Process design** — sales, onboarding, and customer success workflows that match how the team actually works.
- **Team training and handoff** — so the internal operator can maintain things without a retainer.
- **Fractional ops** — ongoing support when the team is not ready to hire a full-time operator yet.

## Best for

Founders and operators at small organizations who want a partner with empathy for "we are a team of four and nobody has time for this." No CRM-industrial-complex.
