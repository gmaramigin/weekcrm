## About 80x

80x builds the "infrastructure-grade" side of Attio — enrichment pipelines, signal-based outbound triggers, and orchestrated AI workflows on top of the Attio data model. Their work sits at the intersection of RevOps and data engineering, and tends to show up in venture firms and startups where the CRM has to keep up with complex deal structures.

## What they do

- **Buyer-signal infrastructure** — product, web, and intent signals wired into Attio.
- **Enrichment pipelines** — Clay, Apollo, internal data sources, and warehouse syncs.
- **AI workflows** — auto-summarization, lead scoring, and next-action recommendations.
- **Complex implementations** for investment firms, portfolio CRM, and multi-fund setups.

## Best for

Teams that want Attio to be the "smart" layer of their GTM — not just a system of record, but an automated engine that surfaces what to do next.
