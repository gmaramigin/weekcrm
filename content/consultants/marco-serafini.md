## About Marco Serafini

Marco is an independent Business Operations Architect who helps agencies and startups connect Attio to the rest of their operating stack. Engagements are small, hands-on, and designed so that the internal team can run things after he is gone — not so they are stuck paying a retainer.

## What they do

- **Attio-centric operations design** — from pipeline to client delivery to finance handoffs.
- **Integration work** — Attio ↔ Notion, Airtable, accounting tools, project management.
- **Playbooks** for agency and consulting businesses specifically.
- **Fractional ops** for small teams that do not need a full-time operator yet.

## Best for

Founder-led agencies and small consultancies who want one senior person to think about their whole system, not a team of juniors following a template.
