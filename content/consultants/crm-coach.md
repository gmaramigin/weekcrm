## About CRM Coach

CRM Coach specializes in guided Attio rollouts and — more usefully for many teams — "rescue projects" where someone has half-implemented Attio, got stuck, and needs a hand unpicking the mess without starting over. They treat training as a first-class deliverable rather than an afterthought.

## What they do

- **Guided rollouts** — step-by-step with real training, not just a handoff document.
- **Rescue projects** for half-done Attio workspaces.
- **Process design** around how the tool gets used day-to-day.
- **Training programs** for both operators and end users.

## Best for

Teams who started setting up Attio internally, hit a wall, and want a partner to pick up where they left off rather than reset everything.
