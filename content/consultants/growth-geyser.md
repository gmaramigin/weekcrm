## About Growth Geyser

Growth Geyser is an Attio-focused consultancy that leans into the custom objects, automation workflows, and AI-driven processes that generalist agencies tend to avoid. They treat Attio less as a contact database and more as an operations engine you program against.

## What they do

- **Custom object modelling** — beyond People/Companies/Deals.
- **Automation workflows** for repetitive GTM motions.
- **AI-driven processes** — enrichment, summarization, routing.
- **Strategic GTM consulting** that connects the tool to the revenue number.

## Best for

Teams who want to push Attio past the default setup — especially if you have a non-standard sales motion or need to model something weird (marketplaces, multi-fund investing, multi-entity consulting).
