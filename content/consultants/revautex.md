I'm Kayla Campos, Founder of Revautex and I design and implement Attio-based GTM systems for teams that have outgrown their CRM or spreadsheets.

With a background in business development and sales, I build systems from the operator's perspective: clean data models, automations that actually run, and reporting your team will use.

## HOW WE CAN HELP:

- Attio setup, migration, and optimization - custom objects, attributes, pipelines, lifecycle logic, workflows, AI attributes / features, and reporting completely tailored to your business
- Integrations via tools like n8n or Zapier - connecting Attio to any platform with an open API
- Training, documentation, and recorded walkthroughs for smooth team adoption

## WHO WE WORK WITH:

Founders, sales teams, and ops teams across SaaS, AI, fintech, agencies, and more, who need a strong GTM foundation without the overhead of a full RevOps hire.

1. **Book Intro Call.** We'll discuss your business, goals and current setup
