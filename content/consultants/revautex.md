## About Revautex

Revautex is founded and led by Kayla Campos, a business development and sales operator who now designs Attio-based GTM systems for teams that have outgrown their CRM or their spreadsheets. The practice's edge is that it builds from the operator's perspective — what a sales team actually needs in the tool, not what a generic implementation template says.

## What they do

- **Attio setup, migration, and optimization** for operator-led teams.
- **Integrations** via n8n or Zapier — including the scrappy ones that bigger agencies skip.
- **Training with recorded walkthroughs** so the team can onboard new hires without another consulting engagement.
- **Ongoing advisory** for founders who want a second brain on their revenue stack.

## Best for

Operator-led teams and founders who want a partner who will speak the language of "how does this help me close more deals this quarter," not "let us rebuild your data model."
