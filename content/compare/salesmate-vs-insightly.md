## TL;DR

- **Pick Salesmate** if calling and texting drive your pipeline and you want native telephony, multi-channel sequences, and sales-marketing-support workflows on one platform.
- **Pick Insightly** if closing a deal is the start of delivery, and you want native project management — tasks, milestones, pipelines — living in the same tool as your sales CRM.

## Communications hub vs delivery hub

These platforms extend the CRM in opposite directions. Salesmate extends it toward *communication*. Built-in calling and SMS mean a rep can dial a prospect, log the call, and fire a follow-up text without leaving the CRM — no third-party dialer. On top of that sit marketing automation and a help-desk module, so sales, marketing, and support share one data layer. It's the platform for teams whose engine is outbound activity: calls, texts, and email cadences.

Insightly extends it toward *delivery*. Its defining move is converting a won opportunity into a project, with tasks, milestones, and delivery pipelines tracked in the same system. Contacts, organizations, opportunities, and projects link together for full context. It's the platform for service businesses — consulting, agencies, construction — where the sale hands off to execution and you want both in one place.

## Pricing

Salesmate Basic starts around $23/user/mo, Pro near $39, and Business near $63, bundling telephony and automation that would otherwise be separate purchases. Insightly is free for up to 2 users, then Plus at $29/user/mo, Professional at $49, and Enterprise at $99, all annual. Insightly's free tier is unbeatable for a two-person shop. In the paid range, Salesmate's Basic undercuts Insightly's Plus and includes a phone system Insightly simply doesn't offer — so if telephony is on your list, Salesmate's bundle is the better value.

## Telephony vs project management — the two dividers

Neither tool does the other's headline feature. Salesmate has native calling and SMS; Insightly has native project management. That asymmetry usually settles the decision faster than any feature matrix. A sales floor that runs on the phone will find Insightly missing its core tool. A consultancy that must track project delivery will find Salesmate stopping at the sale. Identify which of those two capabilities is non-negotiable for you, and the choice is nearly made.

## Automation, AI, and compliance

Salesmate offers a robust automation builder, AI co-pilots for call transcription and summaries, 700+ integrations, and a compliance stack (SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001) that suits regulated industries. Insightly offers strong workflow automation on Professional and up, custom objects on Enterprise, and analytics good enough to earn top-CRM-for-analytics recognition. Both automate well; Salesmate's automation spans communication and support, while Insightly's spans sales and delivery.

## Who should pick what

- **Outbound team that lives on the phone and texts** → Salesmate.
- **Consultancy or agency that delivers projects after the sale** → Insightly.
- **Company wanting sales, marketing, and support on one platform** → Salesmate.
- **Service business needing deal-to-delivery tracking** → Insightly.
- **Regulated industry needing HIPAA/SOC 2 certifications** → Salesmate.
- **Two-person team wanting a free start** → Insightly.
