## TL;DR

- **Pick Gorgias** if you're DTC, ecommerce, or marketplace and your top 10 ticket types are order status, returns, sizing, subscription edits, and shipping. Gorgias's Shopify integration is the deepest in the category and most actions happen in-line in the ticket.
- **Pick Intercom** if you're B2B SaaS, your customers live in your product, and your support strategy depends on in-app chat, AI deflection (Fin), product tours, and proactive messaging.

## Pricing

Gorgias prices by ticket volume: Starter $10/mo (50 tickets), Basic $60 (300), Pro $360 (2,000), Advanced $900 (5,000), with overages and AI Agent / Automate add-ons. Intercom prices by seat plus AI usage: Essential $39/seat, Advanced $99, Expert $139, plus Fin resolutions at $0.99 each. For a small DTC brand at 1,000 tickets/month with 2 agents, Gorgias is ~$200/mo; Intercom is ~$200/mo on Essential before Fin. At 10K tickets/month, Gorgias's volume-priced model scales worse and Intercom's seat-priced model wins on $/ticket — but Intercom doesn't have ecommerce workflows.

## Ecommerce depth

Gorgias's whole reason for existing. The Shopify integration surfaces full order context (line items, payment, fulfillment, customer LTV) on every ticket. Agents can refund, cancel, edit, re-ship, or pause subscriptions without leaving the ticket. Macros can include dynamic order data. Native integrations cover Recharge, Loop Returns, ReturnGo, Shippo, Klaviyo, Yotpo, Gorgias Convert. Intercom can do some of this through apps but treats orders as external data — agents click out to Shopify.

## SaaS / in-app messaging

Intercom's whole reason for existing. Messenger, product tours, checklists, banners, mobile push, and behavior-triggered campaigns are all native. Customers chat with support, sales, and onboarding through the same in-app widget. Intercom's data model treats users as first-class with attributes, events, and segmentation. Gorgias has chat but it's a support widget, not a product-onboarding surface.

## AI and automation

Both have invested heavily in AI in 2025–2026. Intercom Fin is widely considered the strongest helpdesk AI agent — multi-step reasoning, tool use, custom actions, and proven 30–60% deflection on B2B SaaS support content. Gorgias AI Agent is purpose-built for ecommerce: order status, return policy, sizing, shipping ETA. On its target intents, Gorgias AI deflects more — because the questions are more deterministic.

## Channels

Both cover email, chat, SMS, social DMs (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok on Gorgias), and WhatsApp. Gorgias's social coverage is broader and more mature for the ecommerce world. Intercom's chat (Messenger) is the strongest in-product chat experience in the category and includes proactive bot-driven flows that Gorgias doesn't try to match.

## Verdict

Gorgias and Intercom aren't really competitors — they're optimized for different products. Gorgias is ecommerce support infrastructure: tickets, orders, returns, retention. Intercom is in-app engagement infrastructure: chat, onboarding, AI deflection, behavior-driven messaging. If you sell physical goods, Gorgias is the default. If you sell software, Intercom is the default. Teams that try to force one into the other's territory tend to switch within 12 months.
