## TL;DR

- **Pick Breakcold** if your sales motion is social — LinkedIn warm-up, comment-and-DM cycles, multichannel sequences mixing email and LinkedIn touches. Built around the social inbox, not the deal record.
- **Pick Attio** if you want a modern system of record that flexes to any data model — deals, accounts, partnerships, candidates, properties — with AI-native enrichment and a clean UI your team will actually use.

## Pricing

Breakcold starts at $29/mo per user and runs to ~$79/user/mo on the higher tier. Attio offers a free-forever plan up to 3 seats; paid plans run Plus at $34/user/mo, Pro at $69, and Enterprise at $119. Both stay reasonable into mid-market. The decision rarely comes down to price.

## Sales motion fit

This is the cleanest split. Breakcold is built for one thing: warming prospects on LinkedIn (and email), tracking every comment, like, and reply, then converting that engagement into a sales conversation. The product surfaces "who's been engaging with our content this week" as a first-class workflow. Attio is a horizontal CRM — it doesn't have an opinion on your sales motion. You bring the workflow, Attio bends to fit.

## Data model

Attio's headline feature is a flexible, customizable data model where every object is a database. You can model deals, accounts, investments, properties, candidates — anything — without a developer. Breakcold's data model is fixed around contacts, prospects, and pipelines, with custom fields but not custom objects. For a focused outbound motion that's plenty; for unusual workflows, Attio is the only one that fits.

## LinkedIn integration

Breakcold's LinkedIn integration is the deepest of any CRM. It pulls posts, comments, and DMs into a unified social inbox and lets reps engage from inside the CRM. Attio integrates with LinkedIn via Sales Navigator export, the public API, and third-party tools (PhantomBuster, HeyReach), but the engagement layer is something you build. If LinkedIn engagement is core to your motion, Breakcold is years ahead.

## AI and enrichment

Attio ships AI-generated fields out of the box — auto-summarize a record, classify it, enrich from public data — without writing rules. Breakcold's AI features focus on message generation and follow-up suggestions inside the social context. Attio is the broader AI tool; Breakcold's AI is narrower but tuned to the outbound workflow.

## Who should pick what

- **B2B founder running a LinkedIn-led sales motion** → Breakcold. The engagement-tracking workflow is unique.
- **Outbound SDR team layering LinkedIn touches over email sequences** → Breakcold. Multichannel cadences with social activity tracking.
- **VC firm, partnership team, or unusual data model** → Attio. The custom objects make it the only fit.
- **Modern startup that wants a clean, flexible CRM** → Attio. The default product is the best in this category.
- **Team that wants both** → use Attio as the source of truth, Breakcold as the engagement layer, sync via API or Zapier.

## Bottom line

Breakcold and Attio don't really compete head-to-head. Breakcold is a specialized tool for social-led outbound. Attio is a horizontal modern CRM. If LinkedIn is more than 40% of your prospecting, Breakcold pays back its narrowness. If you need a system of record that fits any sales motion, Attio wins.
