## TL;DR

- **Pick LiveAgent** if you want a complete help desk — ticketing, live chat, and a built-in call center — across many channels at per-agent pricing, especially if phone matters.
- **Pick Tidio** if you run an e-commerce store or lean support team and want an AI agent that resolves the bulk of chat inquiries automatically, with deep Shopify/WooCommerce integrations.

## Pricing

LiveAgent starts at $15/agent/mo billed annually, with a 30-day free trial and no credit card needed, billed month-to-month with no contract. Tidio has a free plan and paid plans from $24.17/mo, but its AI agent (Lyro) and advanced Flows are priced as add-ons, so the effective cost climbs quickly for AI-heavy use, and the Plus plan jumps to $749+/mo for team features. LiveAgent's per-agent model is simpler to predict for a growing support team; Tidio can start free but gets expensive fast once you lean on its AI.

## Full helpdesk vs AI chat

LiveAgent is a broad help desk: ticketing, live chat, a built-in call center, knowledge base, and social channels under one subscription. Tidio is narrower and deeper in one area — AI-first live chat. Its flagship Lyro AI agent handles up to 67% of support inquiries automatically, from product details to order status, without a human stepping in. So LiveAgent gives you breadth of channels and a real ticketing backbone; Tidio gives you best-in-class automated chat deflection but a lighter overall help desk.

## E-commerce focus

Tidio is built for B2C and e-commerce. It offers deep native integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, and other platforms, plus no-code Flows for lead capture, discount offers, and support sequences — ideal for stores that want to engage visitors proactively and resolve order questions at scale. LiveAgent is channel-comprehensive and industry-agnostic (clients include Hyundai and NASCAR), but it isn't tuned to e-commerce the way Tidio is. If you run a Shopify store, Tidio's integrations and Lyro are a natural fit.

## Voice and channels

LiveAgent's built-in call center is a key differentiator — native phone support alongside everything else, with social channels including WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Telegram, and Viber (some as paid add-ons). Tidio connects email, live chat, Messenger, Instagram, and WhatsApp into one inbox but has no call center. If phone is part of your support mix, LiveAgent is the only one of the two that covers it.

## Who should pick what

- **Teams needing phone plus tickets and chat** → LiveAgent. The call center plus broad channels.
- **Support teams wanting a predictable per-agent helpdesk** → LiveAgent.
- **Shopify/WooCommerce stores** → Tidio. Native e-commerce integrations and proactive chat.
- **Lean teams wanting AI to deflect most chats** → Tidio. Lyro handles up to 67% automatically.

## Bottom line

These tools optimize for different jobs. LiveAgent is the broader help desk — pick it when you need multiple channels, real ticketing, and especially phone support, with pricing that scales cleanly per agent. Tidio is the sharper tool for automated e-commerce chat — pick it when your priority is deflecting high volumes of store inquiries with AI and you live in the Shopify ecosystem. Watch Tidio's add-on costs if you go AI-heavy, and use LiveAgent's free trial to confirm the call center fits your workflow.
