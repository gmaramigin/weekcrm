## TL;DR

- **Pick Kommo** if your pipeline lives in chat — WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, SMS — and you want a CRM that unifies those conversations into one board with no-code salesbots handling qualification and follow-up.
- **Pick Freshsales** if you run a more traditional sales motion with calls and email, and you want an affordable, AI-assisted CRM with built-in phone, lead scoring, and a free entry tier inside the Freshworks ecosystem.

## Pricing

Freshsales is the more flexible buy: a free plan, paid tiers from around $9/user/mo, and a 21-day trial with no long commitment. Kommo starts at roughly $15/user/mo but requires a six-month minimum — there's no monthly billing — so it asks for upfront commitment. For testing the waters, Freshsales is lower-risk. For a team certain that messaging is their channel, Kommo's commitment is less of an issue.

## Channels: messaging vs phone and email

This is the defining split. Kommo is built for conversational selling: it pulls WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, email, and SMS into a single inbox and pipeline, which makes it a natural fit for markets — much of Latin America and Europe — where deals close in chat threads. Freshsales is built for the classic call-and-email motion: it ships a native phone dialer, email tracking, and a 360-degree timeline per contact. If your customers expect to message you, Kommo. If your reps dial and email, Freshsales.

## Automation & AI

Both automate, differently. Kommo's no-code salesbots qualify leads and run follow-up flows across messaging channels without a developer, plus a built-in AI agent for routing. Freshsales leans on Freddy AI — lead scoring, deal-health insights, email-writing assistance, and forecasting — positioned as a layer on core CRM rather than an add-on. Kommo's automation is conversation-shaped; Freshsales' is pipeline-shaped. Note that Freddy's depth scales with tier, so meaningful AI needs mid-tier or above.

## Ecosystem & scale

Freshsales has the bigger backstop: 74,000+ customers and native ties to Freshdesk and Freshservice, so a growing company can unify sales, support, and IT on one platform. Kommo is more specialized — deep on messaging, lighter on the surrounding business suite. If you anticipate needing support and IT tooling alongside sales, Freshsales' ecosystem is a real advantage. If messaging *is* your business, Kommo's focus is a feature, not a limitation.

## Best fit

Kommo fits SMBs, agencies, and service businesses whose sales happen in DMs and WhatsApp threads. Freshsales fits SMB-to-mid-market sales teams that want capable AI-assisted selling and built-in calling without Salesforce complexity or price — especially anyone already on Freshworks.

## Bottom line

The decision is almost entirely about channel. If your pipeline is conversations on messaging apps, Kommo brings structure without flattening the chat feel — just accept the six-month commitment. If your pipeline runs on calls, email, and a more conventional funnel, Freshsales is more flexible to trial, stronger on AI and telephony, and easier to grow into. Pick the one that matches where your customers actually talk to you.
