## TL;DR

- **Pick Insightly** if you're a service business that needs to track a deal and then deliver the work — its native project management is the differentiator.
- **Pick Zoho CRM** if you want a deeper, more affordable sales CRM with stronger automation and AI, backed by a wide app ecosystem.

## Pricing

Insightly is free for 2 users, then Plus $29, Professional $49, and Enterprise $99 per user/mo. Zoho CRM is free for up to 3 users and runs from $14/user/mo (Standard) to $52/user/mo (Ultimate). Zoho is the cheaper entry point and generally gives you more CRM depth per dollar; Insightly's pricing reflects that you're also buying a projects module, not just a CRM.

## Project management

This is Insightly's reason to exist. When a deal closes, it converts into a project with milestones, tasks, and pipelines for delivery — all inside the same tool, with the original opportunity's data carried over. For consultancies, agencies, and professional-services firms that hate handing closed deals off to a separate PM tool, that closed loop is genuinely useful. Zoho CRM has no equivalent native PM; you'd reach for Zoho Projects as a separate (well-integrated) app.

## Sales features and automation

Zoho CRM is the more complete sales engine: multiple pipelines, Blueprint process automation, Canvas custom layouts, scoring rules, and a mature workflow builder. Insightly covers the sales fundamentals competently — lead routing, opportunities, and pipeline views — but its automation and configurability don't go as deep as Zoho's at comparable tiers.

## AI

Zoho bundles Zia, its AI assistant, for lead and deal scoring, predictions, anomaly detection, and natural-language queries across its mid and upper plans. Insightly's intelligence is lighter, focused on dashboards and reporting rather than predictive AI, so data-driven sales teams will find Zoho's tooling more advanced.

## Ecosystem

Zoho's edge is the surrounding suite — Books, Desk, Campaigns, Analytics, and Zoho One — sharing one login and data layer, so the CRM never becomes an island. Insightly is part of a smaller marketing-and-service family (Insightly Marketing, Service) and leans on integrations like Xero and Google Workspace, which is plenty for an SMB but narrower than Zoho's catalog.

## Bottom line

Insightly is the right call for service businesses whose pain is the deal-to-delivery handoff — having CRM and projects in one place is worth the premium. Zoho CRM is the stronger, cheaper pure-sales platform with better automation, AI, and a larger ecosystem to grow into. If you run projects off your closed deals, choose Insightly; if you want the best sales CRM for the money, choose Zoho.
