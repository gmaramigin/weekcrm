## TL;DR

- **Pick Follow Up Boss** if you already generate leads (Zillow, Realtor.com, your own ads) and need a fast, accountable CRM that routes those leads, enforces follow-up, and plugs into the rest of your stack.
- **Pick BoldTrail** if you want a single platform to *generate* leads and manage them — IDX websites, behavioral lead gen, and CRM in one system — especially at brokerage scale.

## Pricing

Follow Up Boss publishes transparent per-seat pricing. The Grow plan runs about $69 per user/month (around $58 billed annually), Pro is roughly $499/month for up to 10 users, and Platform sits near $833/month (annual) for up to 30 users with cheap incremental seats. Built-in calling and the power dialer are add-ons, typically $25-$50 per user/month. BoldTrail (formerly kvCORE) has no public pricing page and no self-serve trial — you book a demo for a quote. Reported entry pricing starts around $499/month for a solo agent, with team and brokerage tiers climbing steeply and often bundling ad spend. The practical takeaway: FUB is predictable and easy to budget per seat; BoldTrail is quote-based, usually a bigger annual commitment, but you're buying lead generation and websites too, not just a CRM.

## Lead management and routing

This is Follow Up Boss's home turf. Its lead-flow engine ingests leads from 200+ sources and routes them by round-robin, first-to-claim, or direct assignment, with smart lists, accountability reporting, and aggressive follow-up enforcement so nothing rots. Teams using FUB with Zillow Premier Agent reportedly convert noticeably more connections. BoldTrail also routes leads and assigns them across a team, but its CRM is one module in a larger suite rather than a purpose-built accountability machine. Teams that live and die by speed-to-lead and agent accountability tend to find FUB tighter; brokerages that want routing *and* the lead source in the same login lean BoldTrail.

## IDX websites and lead generation

BoldTrail's biggest differentiator is that it *creates* demand. It ships IDX-enabled websites, landing pages, behavioral property-search tracking, and built-in "smart" nurturing that fires based on what a lead actually browses, plus paid-ad and listing tools. Follow Up Boss deliberately does none of this — it has no IDX website builder and no native lead-gen engine. FUB's philosophy is that you bring the leads (or buy them elsewhere) and it makes them convert. If you don't already have a reliable lead source, BoldTrail's all-in-one model can replace several tools at once; if you do, FUB lets you keep your existing sites and ad platforms.

## Integrations and dialer

Follow Up Boss is the more open platform, with a large integration marketplace and deep two-way syncs to Zillow, Realtor.com, transaction tools, and hundreds of apps — it's designed to sit at the center of a best-of-breed stack. Its dialer and texting (often via partner add-ons) are built for high-volume ISA teams hammering call lists. BoldTrail is more of a walled garden: because it bundles website, lead gen, dialer, and CRM, it integrates less with outside tools by design — the value proposition is *not* needing them. If your workflow depends on specific third-party software, verify BoldTrail supports it before committing.

## Brokerage and team scale

BoldTrail, from Inside Real Estate, is built for the enterprise end — large teams and brokerages, with back-office, recruiting, and agent-productivity layers, serving hundreds of thousands of agents across its ecosystem. It's a platform a broker can standardize an entire office on. Follow Up Boss scales well for teams too, with strong admin controls and reporting, but it's a CRM, not a brokerage operating system. A broker wanting one vendor for websites, lead gen, CRM, and back office will find BoldTrail more complete; a team that wants the best CRM and freedom to pick everything else picks FUB.

## Bottom line

Follow Up Boss and BoldTrail solve different problems. FUB is the sharpest pure CRM for converting leads you already have — transparent pricing, open integrations, and relentless follow-up. BoldTrail is an all-in-one engine that generates leads via IDX sites and behavioral marketing and houses them in a brokerage-grade platform. Choose FUB if you have lead flow and want execution; choose BoldTrail if you want the whole machine in one place.
