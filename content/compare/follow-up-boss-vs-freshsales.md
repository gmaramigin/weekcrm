## TL;DR

- **Pick Follow Up Boss** if you run a real estate team and need automatic lead aggregation, built-in calling and texting, and follow-up that never lets a lead slip.
- **Pick Freshsales** if you want an affordable, general-purpose AI sales CRM with Freddy AI built in, usable across any industry.

## Specialist vs generalist

These are both sales CRMs, but they are not competing for the same buyer. **Follow Up Boss is a real estate operating system.** Everything is built around the property industry: it aggregates leads from 250+ sources like Zillow and Realtor.com, routes them to agents automatically, and drives relentless follow-up with built-in calling, texting, and email. 41 of the top 50 U.S. real estate teams use it — a signal of how tightly it fits that one vertical. Outside real estate, its integrations and workflows do not translate.

**Freshsales is a horizontal CRM** from Freshworks with 74,000+ customers spanning SMB to mid-market across many industries. It combines contact management, deal pipelines, and Freddy AI in one product, positioned as a capable, affordable alternative to Salesforce. It goes wide where Follow Up Boss goes deep. The choice is not about quality — it is about whether you want an industry-specific tool or a flexible general one.

## Pricing

The gap here is large. Freshsales offers a **free plan** and paid tiers starting at **$9/user/month**, with a 21-day trial — priced for broad accessibility. Follow Up Boss starts at **$69/user/month (Grow)**, with a **Pro plan at $499/month for 10 users** and a 14-day trial. Follow Up Boss is priced as a specialized team platform, and its Pro plan can carry additional per-user add-on costs for calling. If budget is the deciding factor, Freshsales is dramatically cheaper; Follow Up Boss's price reflects the real-estate-specific machinery you are paying for.

## Lead handling and follow-up

Follow Up Boss's core value is **lead aggregation and follow-up automation**. It pulls from 250+ real estate lead sources, routes each lead to the right agent instantly, and triggers **action plans** — automated drip sequences based on lead source, status, and behavior — so no lead goes cold. Built-in calling and texting keep all outreach in one place. This is purpose-built for the high-volume, speed-to-lead reality of real estate.

Freshsales handles leads well for a general CRM — Freddy AI scores leads, a 360-degree timeline unifies calls, emails, and meetings per contact, and kanban pipelines automate stages — but it does not ingest property-portal leads or ship real-estate follow-up workflows out of the box. For any non-real-estate team, that generality is the point; for a real estate team, Follow Up Boss's ready-made workflows save real setup.

## AI and ecosystem

Freshsales leads on general AI. **Freddy AI** delivers lead scoring, deal recommendations, email composition help, and pipeline forecasting without a separate add-on, and Freshsales integrates natively with Freshdesk and Freshservice for a unified customer platform — strong if you are already in the Freshworks ecosystem. Follow Up Boss's intelligence is narrower and more operational: automation, action plans, and team-performance reporting by agent, lead source, and stage for coaching. Broad AI selling (Freshsales) versus focused real-estate execution (Follow Up Boss).

## Who should pick what

- **Real estate team or brokerage managing portal leads** → Follow Up Boss.
- **General sales team in any other industry** → Freshsales.
- **Budget-conscious team wanting a free or $9 starting point** → Freshsales.
- **Agent team living on calling and texting follow-up** → Follow Up Boss.
- **Company already using Freshdesk or Freshservice** → Freshsales.
- **Team that wants industry workflows ready out of the box** → Follow Up Boss.

## Bottom line

Follow Up Boss is the right call for real estate teams that live on lead speed and follow-up — the 250+ source aggregation and built-in communication are tailored to how agents actually work, and top teams choose it for a reason. Freshsales is the right call for general sales teams that want capable, AI-assisted selling at a fraction of the price. Let your industry decide: real estate points to Follow Up Boss, everything else to Freshsales.
