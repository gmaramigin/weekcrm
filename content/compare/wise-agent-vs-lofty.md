## TL;DR

- **Pick Wise Agent** if you're a solo agent or small team who wants a complete, affordable CRM with transaction management and 24/7 human support at one flat price — and you already have a lead source.
- **Pick Lofty** if you're a growing team that needs IDX websites, managed ad campaigns, a power dialer, and AI nurture in a single platform, and can budget for real-team pricing.

## Pricing

Wise Agent is $49/mo flat — the whole feature set, no per-user charges, with a 14-day free trial and no credit card required. Lofty starts at $449/mo for Core and climbs to $1,500/mo at Enterprise, plus per-user and ad-management fees. That's roughly a 9x difference at entry, and the gap widens once Lofty's add-ons and ad spend are included. Wise Agent's flat model is unusual and valuable: most competitors charge per seat or gate essentials behind upgrades. Lofty's pricing only makes sense if you'll actually use its lead-generation engine.

## What you're actually buying

Wise Agent is a focused CRM: contact management, transaction tracking, email/SMS marketing, landing pages, and a content library — held the Forbes Advisor "Best Real Estate CRM" spot three years running. Lofty is a lead-gen platform with a CRM attached: 33+ built-in lead-gen methods, IDX websites, managed Google/Facebook ads, and a power dialer. The single most important question is whether you need to *generate* leads inside your CRM. Wise Agent doesn't do that — its lead-gen is weak and you'll need a separate source. Lofty is built around exactly that job.

## Transaction management vs AI nurture

Wise Agent's standout is transaction management — contract-to-close checklists, document storage, and task assignment built in, so you don't need a separate tool for it. That's a practical, daily-use strength for agents who care about closing process. Lofty's standout is AI: the AI Workforce for predictive scoring and automated follow-up, behavior-driven Smart Plans, and the 2026 Homeowner Agent that surfaces likely sellers in your existing database. Wise Agent's AI features are deliberately lighter-weight by comparison.

## Support and ease of use

Wise Agent includes 24/7 live human support — phone and chat — at the base tier, a rarity at this price, and onboarding is quick. Its UI feels dated next to newer platforms, but it's functional. Lofty's support reviews are mixed at the SMB tier (better for enterprise accounts), and the platform is wide enough that most teams need 30–60 days to use even 40% of what they pay for. Trading dated-but-simple against modern-but-complex is much of this decision.

## Who should pick what

- **Solo and part-time agents on a budget** → Wise Agent. Flat $49/mo, full feature set.
- **Agents who value transaction management** → Wise Agent. Contract-to-close tooling is built in.
- **Teams needing built-in lead generation** → Lofty. IDX, ads, and 33+ lead-gen methods.
- **Listing-focused or AI-forward teams** → Lofty. The Homeowner Agent is a real differentiator.

## Bottom line

Wise Agent and Lofty sit at opposite ends of the market and rarely overlap in practice. If you have a lead source and want a dependable, cheap CRM with strong transaction tooling and real human support, start with Wise Agent — it does more than its price suggests. If you need a platform to produce and work leads at scale and you'll genuinely use the AI and ad stack, Lofty earns its much higher cost. Don't pay 9x for lead-gen you won't use.
