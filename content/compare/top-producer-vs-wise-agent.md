## TL;DR

- **Pick Wise Agent** if you want a working, full-featured CRM with built-in transaction management and 24/7 live support for one predictable flat fee — ideal for solo and part-time agents.
- **Pick Top Producer** if your volume justifies the spend and you want MLS-driven geographic farming, behavior-aware drip campaigns, and structured team tiers.

## Pricing

This is where the two diverge hardest. **Wise Agent is $49/month flat** — that single price covers the entire feature set, including transaction management, email/SMS marketing, landing pages, and 24/7 live support, with a 14-day no-card trial. Most competitors charge per-user or gate essentials behind upgrades; Wise Agent doesn't.

**Top Producer** runs six tiers. The single-agent Pro plan starts at **$179/user/month**, team plans run **$399–$1,199/month** for 5/10/25 agents, and leads and farming are priced as add-ons. The add-on model (Pro + Leads, Pro + Farming) makes the real monthly cost hard to predict — it's easy to land at $300–$500/month once everything's switched on.

## Farming and lead intelligence

Top Producer's standout is **MLS-driven farming**: targeted outreach to a chosen geography with predictive "likely to sell" scoring pulled from MLS data, plus an AI-assisted follow-up coach. If your strategy is to own a neighborhood and work listings, this is the differentiator.

Wise Agent doesn't compete here. Its lead-generation is intentionally lighter — you'll still need a separate lead source or IDX provider. What it does give you is a polished content library of pre-built marketing assets ready to customize.

## Transaction management

Both include contract-to-close transaction tools, and this is genuinely a strength on both sides. Wise Agent's templates, checklists, and status tracking are a core selling point at its price. Top Producer matches this with close checklists, task tracking, and document handling — but you're paying several times more for the surrounding farming and team features.

## Team features vs. solo simplicity

Top Producer is built to scale into teams, with dedicated 5/10/25-agent tiers and consolidated reporting. Wise Agent tops out earlier — it's excellent up to a handful of agents, then starts to feel under-powered for larger organizations.

Worth noting: both have dated UIs compared to newer entrants like Lofty or BoldTrail. Wise Agent has held the Forbes Advisor "Best Real Estate CRM" spot three years running, which speaks to substance over polish.

## Bottom line

For most **solo and small-team agents, Wise Agent is the smarter buy** — full functionality, transaction management, and live support for a tenth of what a loaded Top Producer plan costs. Step up to **Top Producer** only when MLS farming and structured team tiers genuinely match your business, and run the add-on math carefully before you commit.
