## TL;DR

- **Pick Freshsales** if your team needs AI-guided selling at an affordable price. Freddy AI's scoring and deal recommendations are genuine productivity tools, not just marketing copy, and the $9 entry tier gets you a functional CRM with room to grow.
- **Pick Insightly** if your business model involves closing a deal and then *doing the work* — consulting, professional services, construction, software implementation — and you want one tool that handles both halves without a Zapier bridge to a project app.

## Pricing

Freshsales starts at free (up to 3 users, limited features), with paid plans at roughly $9/user/mo (Growth), $39/user/mo (Pro), and $69/user/mo (Enterprise). Freddy AI's most useful features — deal health scoring, email recommendations — become meaningful at Pro. Insightly's free plan covers 2 users and is genuinely usable for a solo founder; Plus at $29/user/mo is the real entry point for small teams, and Professional at $49/user/mo is where automation opens up. Both require annual billing for best prices. For a 15-person sales team, Freshsales Pro runs about $585/mo; Insightly Professional runs about $735/mo — a ~25% premium for the project layer.

## AI and Automation

Freddy AI is Freshsales' biggest differentiator. It surfaces contact scores, deal health signals, next-best-action prompts, and drafts outbound emails — all within the standard deal and contact views. On Freshsales Pro and above, it's genuinely woven into daily workflow, not a separate module you have to navigate to. Insightly's automation is more traditional: workflow rules trigger based on field changes or stage moves, which is reliable and flexible but doesn't carry the AI-native feel. For teams focused on rep efficiency and pipeline discipline, Freshsales' AI layer delivers measurable value.

## Pipeline and Deal Management

Both platforms offer multi-pipeline support with kanban views, stage automation, and deal forecasting. Freshsales has a cleaner UI for high-velocity sales — drag-and-drop cards, built-in activity feed, and email tracking baked into the deal view at every tier. Insightly's pipeline is equally functional but optimized for considered, longer-cycle deals; the visual pipeline editor is a strength, and the deal-to-project conversion on close is unique. If you run multiple parallel sales motions with different stages and goals, Freshsales handles that more elegantly. If you run one careful pipeline per client type, Insightly's structure is just as good.

## Project Delivery (Post-Sale)

Insightly wins this category outright. Clicking "Won" on a deal triggers a project creation workflow that copies deal data into a project with pre-defined task templates, milestones, and responsible owners. Service businesses that have historically toggled between a CRM and a PM tool (Asana, Basecamp, Monday) find that Insightly's native delivery layer eliminates an entire category of software cost and friction. Freshsales has no equivalent — it ends at deal closure. If you sell services that then require structured delivery, this is the comparison that matters most.

## Integrations and Ecosystem

Freshsales benefits from the broader Freshworks ecosystem: native connections to Freshdesk (customer support), Freshservice (IT), Freshmarketer (marketing), and Freshchat (live chat). If you're building a unified Freshworks stack, the data sharing across products is a genuine advantage. Insightly has strong native integrations with Xero (accounting), Mailchimp, QuickBooks, and a solid Zapier layer, but doesn't have an equivalent platform ecosystem. Both support Zapier and major third-party tools; Freshsales has the larger native integration catalog.

## Reporting

Freshsales' reporting covers standard pipeline metrics, lead source attribution, and Freddy AI-generated forecasts. Insightly earned a 2026 G2 recognition for analytics strength — its report builder is flexible, the project-level reporting is unique (track delivery timelines and milestone completion alongside deal metrics), and custom dashboards are available on Professional and above. If analytics depth across both sales and delivery matters, Insightly has more to offer.

## Verdict

Freshsales and Insightly are solving adjacent but distinct problems. Freshsales is the right call when sales efficiency is the goal — faster reps, better lead prioritization, AI-assisted outreach at an SMB price. Insightly is the right call when the business model requires tracking clients from first contact through final delivery, and you'd rather have one system of record than two. For pure sales teams, Freshsales; for project-based businesses, Insightly.
