## TL;DR

- **Pick Insightly** if your team sells *and* delivers — services businesses, consultancies, agencies — and you want deals to convert into projects in the same system.
- **Pick Pipedrive** if pipeline visibility is the only job and you'd rather have a best-in-class sales UI than a thousand features outside that core.

## Pricing

Insightly: free for 2 users, then Plus at $29/user/mo, Professional at $49, Enterprise at $99. Pipedrive: Essential at $14/user/mo, Advanced at $29, Professional at $59, Power at $79, Enterprise at $99. At equivalent tiers Insightly is meaningfully cheaper, but the comparison is uneven — you're paying for project management you may or may not need.

## Sales pipeline

Pipedrive owns this category. The Kanban-style pipeline is the home page, drag-and-drop is instant, and the reporting that comes off the pipeline is sharper than what Insightly ships. Insightly's pipeline is functional but it's one feature inside a larger platform — not the center of gravity.

## Project management

This is Insightly's home turf. When a deal closes, it converts into a project with milestones, tasks, and deliverables tracked alongside the customer record. For services businesses that hand off from sales to delivery, this is the killer feature — Pipedrive sends the deal to Asana or Monday and the context lives in three places. Insightly keeps it in one.

## Customization

Insightly has more custom objects, fields, and workflows in Professional+. Pipedrive intentionally stays narrow — fields, pipelines, automations, that's mostly it. Teams that want a CRM admin can configure deeply will prefer Insightly; teams that want a CRM that "just works" prefer Pipedrive.

## Integrations

Both have solid marketplaces. Pipedrive's edge is sales-engagement tools (Aircall, JustCall, Outreach, Apollo) and native Slack/Asana. Insightly's edge is Xero/QuickBooks accounting integrations and its native G Suite/Office 365 bundles, which fit the services-business workflow better.

## Mobile

Pipedrive's mobile app is among the best in the category — field-sales reps can actually run their day from it. Insightly's mobile is adequate; field-heavy teams will notice the difference.

## Who should pick what

- **Consultancies, agencies, or any team where a sold deal becomes a delivered project** → Insightly.
- **Pure sales team that wants a pipeline tool and nothing else** → Pipedrive.
- **B2B services on Xero or QuickBooks** → Insightly.
- **Field sales / outside reps who live in mobile** → Pipedrive.
