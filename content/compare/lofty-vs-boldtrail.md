## TL;DR
- **Pick Lofty** if you want the strongest AI lead-engagement assistant, built-in social and PPC ad lead generation, and a polished smartphone-first experience for an individual agent or growing team.
- **Pick BoldTrail** if you run a brokerage or large team that needs deep behavioral automation, back-office and recruiting tooling, and platform-grade scale across many offices.

## Pricing
Neither platform publishes transparent self-serve pricing — both gate quotes behind a sales demo, and your final number depends heavily on team size, add-ons, and ad spend. Lofty is generally tiered by team size, with the AI assistant and ad-driven lead gen often quoted as additional cost. BoldTrail starts higher for an individual seat and rises steeply for teams and brokerages, frequently sold on annual contracts negotiated office-by-office. Treat any figure you see online as a starting point: both are quote-based, and ad budget sits on top of the software fee. The practical takeaway is that Lofty tends to be more accessible to smaller teams, while BoldTrail's pricing reflects its brokerage-scale positioning.

## IDX website and lead generation
Both ship SEO-friendly IDX websites with home valuation and search tools, so the real difference is how leads are captured. Lofty leans hard into paid-ad lead generation: native social (Facebook/Instagram) and PPC ad creation are built in, letting agents launch campaigns and route leads straight into the CRM without a third-party tool. That makes Lofty attractive if you actively buy leads and want one dashboard for spend and follow-up. BoldTrail's strength is breadth — a mature IDX framework with behavioral lead tracking, plus a marketplace of add-ons and lead sources rather than a single opinionated ad engine. For agents who want guided, social-first lead acquisition, Lofty is the more turnkey path; for brokerages standardizing many sites and feeds, BoldTrail's flexibility wins.

## CRM and lead nurture
This is where the two philosophies diverge most. Lofty's CRM is built around its AI assistant, which can text and engage new leads automatically, qualify them, and hand warm conversations back to agents — its Chime heritage shows in approachable workflows and a strong mobile app. BoldTrail centers on behavioral automation: its Smart CRM watches what contacts do on your sites and triggers campaigns and agent alerts from real activity signals, powerful for nurturing large databases over long buying cycles. Want an assistant that proactively works your leads? Lofty is more hands-off. Want a system that reacts to thousands of contacts' behavior? BoldTrail's engine is deeper.

## AI and automation
Lofty has made AI its headline differentiator — the assistant handles conversational follow-up, content help, and smart suggestions, woven into daily agent workflow rather than bolted on. BoldTrail offers AI tooling and keeps expanding it, but its automation reputation rests more on rule-based behavioral triggers and mass-campaign orchestration. Teams that want a "robot ISA" texting leads at 2 a.m. will find Lofty more immediately useful; teams that want granular automation rules across a big pipeline will appreciate BoldTrail's depth.

## Team and brokerage scale
BoldTrail (formerly kvCORE, from Inside Real Estate) is purpose-built for the top of the market. Beyond the CRM it offers BackOffice commission and transaction tooling, recruiting and accountability features, and a unified ecosystem designed to run multi-office brokerages and franchises — which is why large enterprises standardize on it. Lofty scales well into teams with management, routing, and accountability, but its sweet spot is the individual agent up through the mid-sized team, not the 500-agent brokerage. If your decision involves recruiting, back-office, and franchise-wide rollout, BoldTrail is built for that scope; if it's about equipping teams to convert leads faster, Lofty is the sharper tool.

## Bottom line
Both are genuine all-in-one platforms — IDX website, CRM, and lead gen in one login — so the choice is about emphasis, not feature checklists. Lofty wins on AI-driven lead engagement, integrated paid-ad lead generation, and mobile-first usability, the stronger pick for agents and growing teams who live in their phone and buy leads. BoldTrail wins on behavioral automation, back-office and recruiting depth, and brokerage-grade scale, the safer pick for large teams and enterprises. Since both gate pricing behind a demo and add cost for AI and ad spend, get quotes from each against your real team size and lead budget before committing.
