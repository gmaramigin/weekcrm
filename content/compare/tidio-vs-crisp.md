## TL;DR

- **Pick Tidio** if you run an online store and want live chat plus easy-to-build chatbots and AI replies that recover carts and answer FAQs automatically — strong Shopify and ecommerce fit.
- **Pick Crisp** if you're a startup or SaaS team that wants an all-in-one shared inbox tying together chat, email, and social, with a generous feature set at a flat, predictable price.

## Pricing

Tidio has a free plan with limited conversations, then tiers (Starter, Growth, Plus) that scale by seats and conversation volume, plus metered pricing for its Lyro AI agent. Crisp also offers a free tier, with Pro and Unlimited plans priced per workspace rather than strictly per seat, which gets cheaper as your team grows. For a small team that wants many agents, Crisp's flat pricing often wins; for low-volume stores, Tidio's free tier is a strong start.

## Core strengths

Tidio's strength is its visual chatbot builder and ecommerce automations — abandoned-cart flows, product cards in chat, and the Lyro AI agent that resolves common questions. Crisp's strength is breadth as a unified inbox: it bundles chat, a shared team inbox, a CRM-lite contact view, a knowledge base, status pages, and a chatbot builder into one product. Tidio is the sharper conversion tool; Crisp is the broader support platform.

## Ticketing and automation

Both turn conversations into a manageable queue. Crisp's MagicBrowse, co-browsing, and integrated inbox make it feel like a lightweight helpdesk for product teams. Tidio's automations are tuned for triggered messages and chatbot flows that nudge shoppers. Crisp handles multi-channel triage a bit more naturally; Tidio handles proactive, sales-oriented automation better.

## Channels and integrations

Tidio integrates tightly with Shopify, WooCommerce, and other ecommerce platforms, and connects Messenger and Instagram. Crisp connects email, Messenger, WhatsApp, Telegram, Line, and Twitter/X into one inbox, plus a good API and a public plugin marketplace. If your customers live across messaging channels, Crisp consolidates more of them.

## Reporting

Both provide conversation volume, response-time, and satisfaction metrics. Tidio adds ecommerce-flavored analytics around chatbot performance and revenue influenced; Crisp adds team-performance and inbox analytics suited to support operations.

## Bottom line

Choose Tidio if you're selling online and want chat plus conversion-focused bots. Choose Crisp if you want one affordable, multi-channel inbox for a growing startup. Both have free plans — install each on a test page and see which matches your channels and volume.
