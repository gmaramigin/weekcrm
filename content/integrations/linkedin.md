LinkedIn is the system of record for most B2B contacts in 2026, but LinkedIn itself isn't a CRM and never will be. The vendors below close that gap — most ship a Chrome extension that captures profiles into the CRM in one click, and the better ones sync LinkedIn messages and conversation history into the contact record automatically. If your team prospects on LinkedIn, this integration is the difference between a usable CRM and a graveyard of stale contacts.
