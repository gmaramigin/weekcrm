"AI CRM" was a marketing term in 2024 and is a real product category in 2026. The CRMs below ship AI features that change daily workflow: automatic field enrichment, predictive lead scoring, conversation intelligence, agentic follow-up, and meeting transcription that flows back into the record. The bar to make this list is "AI is a first-class feature, not a sidebar widget" — most vendors below either started AI-native or rebuilt their core around it.
