If your team lives in Outlook, the CRM you pick should too. The vendors below offer native Microsoft Outlook integration — two-way email sync, calendar sync, contact sync, and (in most cases) an Outlook add-in that lets reps log activity, view CRM context, and update records without leaving the inbox. Watch out for "Outlook integration" that's really just IMAP — true two-way sync via Microsoft Graph is the bar.
