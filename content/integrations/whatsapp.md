If WhatsApp is a primary support or sales channel for your customers, your CRM has to treat it as a first-class inbox — not an afterthought. The vendors below offer native WhatsApp Business API integration: two-way messaging from inside the CRM, shared team inboxes, conversation history attached to the contact record, and (in most cases) WhatsApp template messaging for outbound at scale. The right pick depends on whether WhatsApp is your support channel (favor the help-desk vendors below) or your sales channel (favor the conversational-CRM vendors).
