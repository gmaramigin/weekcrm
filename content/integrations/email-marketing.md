## Why a CRM with native email marketing matters

The traditional setup is a separate ESP (Mailchimp, Klaviyo, ActiveCampaign) syncing into a CRM via Zapier or a native connector. It works, and it's still the right answer if your email program is the center of gravity for the business. But for sales-led teams, founder-led startups, and small marketing functions, running email marketing inside the CRM avoids three problems: contacts double-bouncing between systems, attribution gaps when a lead converts, and the per-contact pricing wall that ESPs build around list growth.

## What to prioritize

- **Native broadcasts and nurture sequences.** Both — broadcasts for announcements, sequences for onboarding/sales drips.
- **Template editor that doesn't require HTML.** Block-based editor with brand kits beats a 1998 rich-text box.
- **Deliverability infrastructure.** Custom domain authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), sender reputation monitoring, and bounce handling are baseline. Some CRM-bundled email tools cut corners here.
- **Send-time analytics.** Opens, clicks, bounces, unsubs, and revenue attribution per send. If you can't tie an email to pipeline created, the integration isn't doing its job.
- **Segmentation on CRM data.** "Send to everyone who hit demo last quarter and is on the Pro plan" should be one query, not a CSV export round trip.
- **Compliance and unsubscribe handling.** Unified suppression list across CRM and email. CAN-SPAM and GDPR footers handled automatically.

## When this is the right call

- **Sales-led teams sending newsletter, sales nurture, and event invites from one system** — the simplicity wins.
- **Small marketing teams (1–3 people) at SMB and mid-market** — one tool, one bill, one user experience to learn.
- **Founder-led startups before hiring a marketer** — don't buy a separate ESP until the email program justifies the SKU.

## When it isn't

- **High-volume B2C senders (>500K/month)** — get a real ESP. Klaviyo, Customer.io, or Iterable will outperform any CRM-bundled email tool on deliverability and segmentation.
- **Sophisticated lifecycle marketing** — multi-channel orchestration, behavioral triggers, and complex branching belong in a dedicated tool.
- **Transactional email** — that's a different category entirely (Postmark, SendGrid, Resend).
