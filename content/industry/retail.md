Retail and DTC support is unlike B2B SaaS support in three concrete ways: ticket volume is 5–10x higher per dollar of revenue, every conversation is downstream of an order or product question, and customer LTV depends on resolution speed more than depth. The CRMs and helpdesks below either ship native Shopify/BigCommerce/WooCommerce integrations that surface orders, refunds, and subscriptions inside every conversation, or they pair cleanly with a CRM-style data layer for loyalty and lifecycle marketing. If your support agent has to switch tabs to look up an order, the tool is the wrong shape.
