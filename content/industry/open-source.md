Open-source CRMs trade convenience for control: you run them on your own infrastructure, customize them at the source level, and pay zero per-seat fees. The trade-off is real ops cost — backups, upgrades, monitoring, and security are now your problem. The CRMs below are the most mature open-source options in 2026, ranging from full SuiteCRM-style platforms to lighter options for teams with strict data-residency requirements.
