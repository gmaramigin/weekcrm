Startup CRMs need a different shape than enterprise CRMs: a free tier that survives the first 5 hires, a UI that doesn't need training, and clean export paths for the day you outgrow it. The vendors below all clear that bar. Most offer a free or near-free entry tier, and the ones that don't (Close, Pipedrive) compensate with fast time-to-value and no implementation overhead.
