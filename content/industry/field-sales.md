Field sales CRMs live or die on three things: a mobile experience that works offline (cell coverage in territory is unpredictable), fast record entry from a phone (anything more than 10 seconds and reps will skip it), and route or territory planning that respects the way outside reps actually work. The vendors below either ship those capabilities natively or have a mature mobile app that field reps don't fight with.
