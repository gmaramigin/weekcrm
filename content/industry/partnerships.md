Partnership and business development CRM needs differ from direct sales in several important ways: deal cycles are measured in months or quarters, not days; the relationships are bilateral (both parties bring value to the deal); and the pipeline tracks not just accounts but the health of the partner relationship itself. Most standard CRMs model this poorly — there's no native concept of a co-sell opportunity where two orgs share pipeline visibility, or a referral that needs attribution back to the partner who sourced it. Teams running partner programs typically work around this with custom objects, partner-specific pipeline stages, and a spreadsheet that tracks what the CRM can't. The vendors below have either built native partnership data models or are commonly customized for partner-led growth — look for joint pipeline visibility, partner portal support, and the ability to track referral source attribution without manual workarounds.
