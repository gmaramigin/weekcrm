Pharma and life sciences CRM lives in a different regulatory universe than commercial CRM. The buyer is an HCP (healthcare professional), the rep can't ship samples without a signed PDMA receipt, every detail must be logged for Sunshine Act reporting, and the system itself often needs to be a validated GxP-compliant deployment. The CRMs below are either purpose-built for life sciences (Veeva sets the bar; the rest define themselves against it) or are general platforms with the configurability and compliance posture to deploy in a pharma environment. If your firm is field-sales-led with a sample budget and HCP targeting, the standard-issue B2B CRM will not pass an audit — pick from the shortlist below.
