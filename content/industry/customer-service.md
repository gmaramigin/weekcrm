Customer service tooling has fragmented into two distinct categories that are increasingly being asked to work as one: the helpdesk (ticket management, SLA tracking, shared inbox) and the CRM (account records, relationship history, revenue context). The best customer service platforms in 2026 blur that line — a support agent should be able to see the customer's contract value, open renewal, and relationship owner alongside the ticket they're resolving, without switching tools. The vendors below either ship that unified view natively or integrate closely enough that the two-tool approach doesn't create friction. For teams choosing their first platform: prioritize the channel where your customers actually reach you (email, chat, phone, in-app) and work backwards to the data model, rather than starting with features and discovering the channel coverage late.
