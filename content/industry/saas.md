SaaS sales motions live or die on three things: clean PQL routing from in-app behavior, support for usage-based or hybrid deal structures, and a marketing-sales handoff that doesn't leak revenue at the seam. The CRMs below either solve those out of the box or integrate cleanly with the SaaS stack — Stripe, Segment, Customer.io, Slack, Linear, Intercom — that most SaaS teams already run.
