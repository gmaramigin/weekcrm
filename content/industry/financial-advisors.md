Financial advisor CRMs solve three problems generic CRMs don't: clients aren't deals that close once, they're decade-long relationships that span multiple legal entities; a regulator can ask for a record at any time, so audit trails and email archiving are not optional; and the surrounding tech stack — custodians, portfolio management, financial planning, e-signature — is more interconnected than most B2B SaaS, so integration coverage matters more than feature checkboxes. The vendors below either ship advisor-native workflows or have battle-tested integrations into the RIA ecosystem.
