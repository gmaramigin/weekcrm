An ABM-ready CRM has to model accounts as first-class objects with hierarchies (parent/subsidiary, named-account tiering, territory ownership), multi-stakeholder buying committees with role and influence captured per contact, and the ability to ingest intent signals — from 6sense, Demandbase, Clearbit, or RB2B — directly onto the account record so reps see warm-up signals next to pipeline. Most generic CRMs handle accounts and contacts but flatten the data model in a way that ABM teams have to compensate for with custom objects, manual playbooks, or a separate ABM platform glued on top. The vendors below either ship the account-based model natively (Attio, Salesforce, HubSpot Enterprise) or integrate so deeply with the modern ABM stack that the integration cost is negligible. For a ranked shortlist of ABM-ready CRMs, see [Best CRM for ABM](/best/best-crm-for-abm).
