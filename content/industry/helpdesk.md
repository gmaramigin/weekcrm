Helpdesk software in 2026 is a wider category than it used to be. The traditional ticketing stack (queue, status, assignee, SLA) is still the operational core, but the buying conversation now rotates around AI deflection rates, multi-channel coverage, and how cleanly the platform integrates with the rest of the customer record. The vendors below cluster into four shapes — full-suite enterprise platforms, AI-first conversational tools, collaborative inboxes for teams that hate ticketing, and vertical-specific helpdesks built for ecommerce or B2B SaaS.
