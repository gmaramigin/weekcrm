Coaches and course creators run a fundamentally different pipeline than a sales team. The "deal" is a coaching engagement or a cohort enrollment — high-touch, relationship-led, often paid via subscription or installment, and tightly coupled to scheduling and content delivery. A traditional sales CRM with deal stages and forecasting tends to feel over-engineered; what works better is a clientflow tool that handles intake, contracts, scheduling, payments, and ongoing client communication in one place. The vendors below either purpose-build for service businesses (HoneyBook, Bonsai, vCita) or are flexible enough that a solo coach can model the workflow without configuring a sales pipeline they don't have. For larger coaching businesses (group programs, masterminds, paid communities), a more conventional CRM (Attio, HubSpot, Pipedrive) starts to make sense once the operations cross 50+ active clients and ad-hoc tracking stops scaling.
