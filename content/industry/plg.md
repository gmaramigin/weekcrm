PLG CRMs are different from traditional sales CRMs in three ways: the customer record starts the moment a user signs up — not when a sales rep first calls them — so product activity has to live alongside contact details; the deal pipeline is gated by product-qualified lead signals (usage thresholds, feature adoption, team-size growth) rather than rep-driven outbound; and the sales motion is almost always a hybrid — self-serve users converting to expansion deals managed by a CSM or AE. Traditional CRMs treat all three as bolt-ons; the vendors below either build for this motion natively or integrate so cleanly with the data layer (Segment, Customer.io, Hightouch) that product signals flow into the CRM without engineering work.
