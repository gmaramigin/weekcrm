Fintech is the vertical where commercial CRM choices are most often wrong by default. Three things shape the requirement set: **regulatory record-keeping** (every customer interaction may need to survive an audit, FINRA exam, or state-level money-transmission review), **identity and KYC data** that lives next to commercial CRM data but can't leak into it, and **integration with the banking-as-a-service stack** — Plaid, Stripe Treasury, Modern Treasury, Unit, Marqeta, Lithic — where a CRM that doesn't speak that ecosystem will require a custom data layer to be useful. The CRMs below are either deployed broadly across fintech (with deep audit logs, role-based permissions, and clean integrations to the modern fintech stack) or have the data-model flexibility to bend into a fintech shape without forcing the firm to fit the tool.
