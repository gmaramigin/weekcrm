Insurance CRMs need three things general CRMs handle poorly: policy and renewal tracking (a deal that never closes — it just renews on a schedule), AMS integration (Applied, Vertafore, HawkSoft, EZLynx — the CRM has to play nicely with the AMS-of-record, not replace it), and compliance-aware data handling (HIPAA for health, GLBA for life, retention rules for P&C). The vendors below either ship insurance overlays or integrate cleanly alongside an existing AMS without data conflicts. For a ranked shortlist, see [Best CRM for Insurance Agents](/best/best-crm-for-insurance-agents).
