## Why look for a Crisp alternative

Crisp is a smart Intercom alternative for small teams — an omnichannel inbox, AI agents, and a knowledge base on flat per-workspace pricing that doesn't balloon as you add seats. But it has clear limits. The CRM is lightweight (contact history and segmentation, not a real sales pipeline), co-browsing and advanced automation sit behind higher tiers, and it's a support-first tool that isn't built for complex B2B sales. Teams that need deeper AI, richer workflows, or a more polished single-channel chat experience often outgrow it.

## How we picked

We weighted AI quality, channel coverage, pricing model, and fit for support style — from lean small-team chat to complex cross-department operations. Each pick targets a specific Crisp gap.

## What to consider

- **You want best-in-class AI support** → **Intercom**. The Fin AI agent leads on resolution rates and you pay $0.99 per issue resolved, plus strong in-product messaging. Seats run $29–$132/month; model usage-based costs before committing.
- **You want flat-friendly value with AI** → **Tidio**. A generous free plan, no-code Flows, and the Lyro agent (up to 67% of inquiries handled). The closest small-team swap, though Lyro and advanced Flows are add-ons.
- **Real-time website chat is your priority** → **LiveChat**. A polished, dedicated chat platform with AI reply suggestions and visitor tracking, transparent per-agent pricing from $19/month. ChatBot is a separate add-on.
- **You want support to feel personal** → **Help Scout**. An email-style shared inbox, an AI assistant that resolves ~70% of routine requests, and the Beacon widget. Free for up to 5 users, paid from $25/user/month.
- **Your issues cross departments** → **Front**. A shared inbox built for complex, multi-channel operations where a request touches sales, fulfillment, and support — with cross-team workflows and AI Autopilot. From $25/user/month at Starter.

## Bottom line

If you're scaling support, **Intercom** offers the strongest AI and **Front** the best cross-team workflows. Stay value-led with **Tidio**, go dedicated-chat with **LiveChat**, or choose **Help Scout** for a warm, email-style experience. Trial two against real conversations for a week before you migrate off Crisp.
