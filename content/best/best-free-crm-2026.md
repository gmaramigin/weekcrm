## How we picked

"Free" CRM means different things across vendors. Some give you a full free-forever plan with seat limits, some offer a generous free trial that expires, and some hide the free tier behind a credit card. Every pick below has a **genuinely free-forever plan** (no expiry, no card required) that's usable for a real sales process — not a teaser tier that dies in two weeks.

## What to consider

- **Most generous free plan, period** → HubSpot. Unlimited users, 1M contacts, a usable pipeline, email tracking, and meeting links. The upsell is real, but the free tier alone runs a small sales team.
- **Best modern free CRM** → Attio. Free for up to 3 seats, with the full flexible data model, AI fields, and Chrome extension. The right answer for early-stage startups.
- **Best free all-in-one suite** → Zoho CRM. Free for up to 3 users with leads, contacts, deals, and tasks; the Zoho One ecosystem is the upgrade path.
- **Best free plan for unlimited users** → Bitrix24. Free for unlimited users with caps on storage and feature depth — the right answer for teams of 10+ that can't justify any per-seat spend yet.
- **Cheapest free-trial-then-cheap pipeline CRM** → Capsule. 14-day free trial, then $9/user/mo Starter — the lowest functional price point in the category.
- **Free starter for relationship-led founders** → Folk. Free plan covers contact import from LinkedIn, Gmail, X — enough to validate the network-graph model before paying.

## Pricing snapshot

Free CRMs make their money in three places: **paid seats** (exceeding the free seat cap), **paid features** (automation, advanced reporting, API access), and **paid contact volume** (HubSpot's marketing tier in particular). Read the upgrade conditions on each free plan carefully — the right free CRM is the one whose paywalls match the things you'll never need.

## Trial advice

For free CRMs, the honest test isn't whether you can use them today — it's whether the free tier still works at month 6. Pick two finalists, import 200 contacts and 20 deals into each, and let them run for two weeks. If you've already hit a feature paywall, the free plan was a trial, not a tier — pick differently.
