## Why look for a Top Producer alternative

Top Producer is one of the longest-running CRMs in real estate, and it earned that longevity honestly: its MLS-driven "likely to sell" farming and smart drip campaigns are genuinely good for the listing-focused agent who wins by owning a neighborhood. But longevity has a downside. The interface can feel dated next to newer, automation-first CRMs; leads and farming are paid add-ons rather than baked in; and per-seat pricing adds up once a team grows. Agents who started on Top Producer often outgrow the *feel* of it before they outgrow the function.

The right alternative depends on what you actually want to upgrade — the experience, the lead generation, or the price.

## How to choose

If you want a modern follow-up experience above all, **Follow Up Boss** is the cleanest upgrade: it ingests leads from 250+ sources, routes them in seconds, and drives relentless follow-up with calling, texting, and action plans — the category benchmark for inside-sales teams. If you'd rather consolidate everything, **Lofty** and **BoldTrail** are the two all-in-ones to weigh: both bundle a CRM, IDX website, lead-gen tools, and a dialer, so you stop paying for farming and leads as separate add-ons. **Lofty** leans into AI and seller-intent mining; **BoldTrail** is the more established brokerage-scale option.

For solo agents, the move is usually down-market, not up. **Wise Agent** delivers the core CRM, transaction management, and marketing at a flat rate that a one-agent book can justify — a three-time Forbes Advisor "Best Real Estate CRM." And if your business is built on referrals and repeat clients rather than farming for new listings, **Realvolve** goes deeper on behavior-triggered workflows and relationship nurture than Top Producer's drip campaigns, without forcing a lead or IDX stack you'd have to pay for.

## Bottom line

Stay on Top Producer if its farming engine is the heart of your business and the dated feel doesn't bother you. Otherwise, match the upgrade to the itch: **Follow Up Boss** for modern follow-up, **Lofty** or **BoldTrail** for an all-in-one that includes lead gen, **Wise Agent** for an affordable solo CRM, or **Realvolve** for referral-driven workflow automation. The lighter tools go live in days and most offer a trial — load your top two with real contacts and run a week of real activity before you commit.
