## How we picked

B2B sales has a specific shape: pipelines with multi-touch deals, account-based motions, and a rep workflow that lives across email, calls, LinkedIn, and the CRM. The best B2B CRMs solve three things — **pipeline UX reps will actually use**, **integrations with the sales-engagement stack** (Apollo, Outreach, Aircall, Gong, ZoomInfo), and **reporting that surfaces conversion and velocity, not just activity counts**. Every pick below delivers all three. We skipped vendors that are clearly built for B2C, ticket support, or services-business workflows.

## What B2B sales teams should prioritize

- **Pipeline-first home view.** Reps live in the pipeline. If it's not the home screen, adoption suffers.
- **Native or first-class dialer.** Outbound teams place 50–100+ calls/day. A dialer that isn't tightly integrated kills velocity.
- **Email sequences and tracking.** Multi-touch B2B prospecting needs sequences inside the CRM or one click away.
- **Custom objects.** B2B deals reference accounts, contacts, partners, and channel partners — the schema must flex.
- **Reporting on velocity, conversion, and win rate** — not just open activities.
