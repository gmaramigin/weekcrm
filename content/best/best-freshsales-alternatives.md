## Why look for a Freshsales alternative

Freshsales punches above its price — Freddy AI, a 360-degree customer view, and kanban pipelines for as little as $9/user/month. But the entry plan is limited: advanced automation and reporting are gated behind higher tiers, Freddy AI's depth varies by plan, and customization is less flexible than enterprise CRMs for non-standard sales processes. Teams that want native calling, a unified marketing-and-support stack, or simply more pipeline polish for the money tend to compare options.

## How we picked

We prioritized CRMs that match or beat Freshsales on value, with fewer features locked behind upsells, plus stronger pipeline UX, calling, or platform breadth. Pricing transparency and time-to-value mattered too — Freshsales users are generally cost-conscious SMB and mid-market teams.

## What to consider

- **You want the simplest pipeline** → **Pipedrive**. A visual, drag-and-drop pipeline from $14/user/month, with an AI Sales Assistant in every paid plan and 300+ integrations. Cleaner and more focused than Freshsales for pure sales work.
- **You also need marketing** → **HubSpot**. Bundles sales, marketing, service, and CMS, with the category's most generous free CRM. Watch the jump from Starter ($20) to Professional ($100 + $1,500 onboarding).
- **You want maximum depth for the money** → **Zoho CRM**. Salesforce-like configurability — custom modules, Blueprint process management, Zia AI — from $14/user/month, free for 3 users. The closest like-for-like on price with more headroom.
- **You're an outbound team** → **Close**. Native power dialer, SMS, and email sequences from $19 to $129/user/month. If your reps make 30+ calls a day, this beats Freshsales' lighter calling tools.
- **You want one platform for sales, marketing, and support** → **Salesmate**. Built-in calling and SMS plus marketing automation and a help desk on a shared data layer, from $23/user/month. A strong consolidation play to cut tool sprawl.

## Bottom line

If Freshsales' tier-gating is the issue, **Zoho CRM** or **Pipedrive** give you more for less. Pick **HubSpot** if you need marketing under one roof, **Close** for high-volume calling, and **Salesmate** to consolidate sales, marketing, and support. Run your top two side by side for a week and keep the one your reps actually use.
