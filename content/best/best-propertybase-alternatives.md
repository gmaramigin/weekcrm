## Why look for a Propertybase alternative

Propertybase took a different path from most real estate CRMs: instead of building a purpose-made platform, it built on top of Salesforce, inheriting near-unlimited customization, enterprise-grade reporting, and the flexibility to model almost any brokerage workflow. For a large, technically resourced brokerage, that's genuinely powerful. The trade-off is everything that comes with Salesforce — complexity, a real admin burden, lengthy implementation, and a cost structure that climbs as you configure. Many teams discover they're paying for flexibility they never use and an administration overhead they didn't sign up for.

The question is whether you actually need a customizable platform, or a real estate system that works the way agents work without a consultant in the loop.

## How to choose

If you want brokerage-scale capability without the Salesforce overhead, **BoldTrail** (the kvCORE successor) is the most direct swap: a purpose-built all-in-one with CRM, IDX websites, and 20+ lead-gen tools, adopted across major brokerage networks and configured for real estate out of the box. **Lofty** is the AI-forward alternative — same all-in-one promise, with stronger automation and seller-intent mining and a built-in dialer.

If the part of Propertybase you care about is the lead pipeline and you'd rather a vendor *run* paid acquisition, **BoomTown** delivers the managed Facebook and Google funnel end to end. For teams that already have leads flowing and simply want the best follow-up in the category — without standing up a whole platform — **Follow Up Boss** routes leads in seconds and drives relentless follow-up, no admin required. And a listing-led team that wins by farming should look at **Top Producer**, whose MLS-driven "likely to sell" signals and drip campaigns target that motion directly.

## Bottom line

Stay on Propertybase only if you genuinely need deep customization and have the admin resources to run it. Otherwise, trade configurability for purpose-built simplicity: **BoldTrail** or **Lofty** for an all-in-one that works out of the box, **BoomTown** for managed paid leads, **Follow Up Boss** if you already have leads and want elite follow-up, or **Top Producer** if you live on listings. Insist on a reference call with a brokerage your size and pilot your top two before migrating — platform switches are heavy, so validate the workflow with real activity first.
