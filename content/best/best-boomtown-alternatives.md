## Why look for a BoomTown alternative

BoomTown built its reputation on doing the hardest part of real estate for you: running the Facebook and Google campaigns, capturing leads on branded IDX sites, scoring them predictively, and feeding them into a CRM your team works. For a team where paid lead generation already converts, that end-to-end funnel is genuinely valuable. The catch is the math. Between the software fee and the required ad spend, an all-in BoomTown setup realistically runs into the thousands per month — and that only makes sense if your cost-per-acquisition is already healthy.

Teams typically start shopping for alternatives for one of two reasons: the all-in cost has outgrown the return, or they want to own their lead generation rather than rent a managed funnel they can't fully see into.

## How to choose

If you want to keep lead gen in-house but run the tools yourself, **BoldTrail** (the kvCORE successor behind major brokerages) and **Lofty** both bundle IDX websites, 20+ lead-gen tools, a CRM, and a dialer — the difference from BoomTown is that you operate the campaigns instead of paying a vendor to manage them. **Lofty** leans hardest into AI, using your existing database to surface seller intent rather than relying solely on new paid buyer leads.

If your real conclusion is that paid lead gen isn't your engine, change categories entirely. **Follow Up Boss** assumes you already have a lead source and focuses everything on routing, speed-to-lead, and relentless follow-up — the discipline that actually converts leads you've already paid for. **Top Producer** is built for the listing agent who wins by owning a neighborhood: MLS-driven "likely to sell" farming and drip campaigns instead of buyer ads. And for a larger brokerage that wants to design its own lead and pipeline workflows, **Propertybase** is built on Salesforce and trades packaged simplicity for deep customization.

## Bottom line

BoomTown is worth it only if paid lead gen already works and the all-in cost returns more than it consumes. If it doesn't, name the real gap: **BoldTrail** or **Lofty** to run lead gen yourself, **Follow Up Boss** to convert leads you already have, **Top Producer** to farm for listings instead of buying ads, or **Propertybase** for brokerage-scale customization. Before signing anything with an ad commitment, get a reference call with a team your size and budget honestly for the spend — that's the line item that decides whether any managed funnel is worth it.
