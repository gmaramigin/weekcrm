## Why look for a Lofty alternative

Lofty — the platform formerly known as Chime — is one of the more aggressive all-in-one plays in real estate: AI lead nurture, IDX websites, ad management, a power dialer, and a 2026 "Homeowner Agent" that mines your existing database for seller intent, all under one login. For a growth-minded team that wants the whole funnel in one place, it's compelling. But the same all-in-one model that makes it attractive also makes it pricey: a base platform fee, per-user charges, and ad spend on top can add up faster than teams expect, and the sheer breadth is more than a focused operation needs.

So the real question is whether you want another full AI all-in-one, or a sharper tool for the specific job — lead gen, follow-up, or listings — that's actually driving your revenue.

## How to choose

If you want to stay all-in-one but prefer a more established, brokerage-backed platform, **BoldTrail** (the kvCORE successor behind RE/MAX, eXp, and BHHS) is the closest like-for-like: CRM, IDX, 20+ lead-gen tools, and a dialer, with deeper enterprise adoption. If the part of Lofty you value most is lead generation and you'd rather a vendor *run* the campaigns than manage them yourself, **BoomTown** does the paid funnel end to end and scores leads before they hit the CRM.

For teams that already have a working lead source, **Follow Up Boss** is the focused counterpoint: it skips the website and lead gen entirely and pours everything into routing, speed-to-lead, and follow-up accountability — the best in the category. If your business is built on listings, **Top Producer** attacks farming directly with MLS-driven "likely to sell" signals and drip campaigns. And a larger brokerage that wants to design its own workflows rather than adopt a packaged platform should evaluate **Propertybase**, built on Salesforce for deep, custom configurability.

## Bottom line

Stay on Lofty if you use the full AI stack and the per-user-plus-ads pricing returns more than it costs. Otherwise, match the tool to the gap: **BoldTrail** for a more established all-in-one, **BoomTown** for managed paid leads, **Follow Up Boss** if you already have leads and need follow-up, **Top Producer** if you live on listings, or **Propertybase** for brokerage-scale customization. Run your top two with real contacts for a week, and budget honestly for any ad spend before you sign.
