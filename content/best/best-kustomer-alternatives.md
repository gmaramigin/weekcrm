## Why look for a Kustomer alternative

Kustomer is a serious AI-first customer service platform — a unified customer timeline, omnichannel coverage, AI agents, and custom objects for complex product and order data. It's a strong fit for high-volume retail, travel, and consumer brands. But it's a heavy investment: pricing is not public and requires a sales conversation, annual contracts are standard, voice and WhatsApp are billed separately on a pay-as-you-go basis, and the platform's depth means onboarding takes real time and resources. Teams that want transparent pricing, faster setup, or a lighter footprint look at the options below.

## How we picked

We weighted omnichannel coverage, AI quality, pricing transparency, time-to-value, and fit by support model (ecommerce, SaaS, cross-team operations). Each pick can carry serious support volume while being easier to budget or deploy than Kustomer.

## What to consider

- **You want a proven, scalable all-rounder** → **Zendesk**. Ticketing, chat, voice, and help center in one Suite (from $55/agent/month), mature automation, and 1,000+ integrations — with transparent pricing Kustomer doesn't offer. Watch total cost as add-ons accumulate.
- **You're a Shopify ecommerce brand** → **Gorgias**. Purpose-built for ecommerce, with live order data inside tickets, ticket-based pricing from $10/month, and an AI Agent for routine inquiries. The natural fit for the retail and consumer-goods brands Kustomer also targets.
- **Your issues cross departments** → **Front**. A shared inbox for complex, multi-channel operations where a request touches sales, fulfillment, and support — cross-team workflows, SLA tracking, and AI Autopilot, from $25/user/month.
- **You're a SaaS product** → **Intercom**. AI-first support with the Fin agent (industry-leading resolution, $0.99 per resolved ticket) and best-in-class in-product messaging. Seats run $29–$132/month.
- **You want simple and human** → **Help Scout**. An email-style shared inbox with an AI assistant resolving ~70% of routine requests. Free for up to 5 users, paid from $25/user/month — the lightest, fastest-to-deploy option here.

## Bottom line

For most teams leaving Kustomer, **Zendesk** is the safest scalable choice with pricing you can actually budget. Pick **Gorgias** for Shopify, **Front** for cross-team operations, **Intercom** for AI-first SaaS support, and **Help Scout** when you want simplicity over depth. Trial two with real ticket volume for a week before migrating.
