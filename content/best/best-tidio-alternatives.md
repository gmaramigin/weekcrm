## Why look for a Tidio alternative

Tidio is one of the most accessible AI support tools out there — a genuinely useful free plan, no-code Flows, and the Lyro AI agent that handles up to 67% of inquiries. But the value equation shifts as you grow: Lyro and advanced Flows are priced as add-ons, so AI-heavy use gets expensive, the Plus plan ($749+/month) is a steep jump, and the product is built more for B2C and ecommerce than B2B account management. Teams that need deeper support workflows, different channels, or more predictable pricing tend to look around.

## How we picked

We weighted AI quality, channel coverage, pricing predictability, and fit for support model (ecommerce vs. SaaS vs. general support). Each pick addresses a specific Tidio shortcoming — whether that's AI depth, B2B fit, or full ticketing.

## What to consider

- **You're a SaaS product wanting better AI** → **Intercom**. The Fin AI agent has industry-leading resolution rates and you pay $0.99 per issue actually resolved, plus best-in-class in-product messaging. Seats run $29–$132/month; budget for usage-based AI.
- **You want Tidio's value with predictable pricing** → **Crisp**. Omnichannel inbox (chat, email, WhatsApp, Instagram), AI agents, and a knowledge base on flat per-workspace pricing from $45/month — no per-seat balloon. The closest value-led swap, with a free plan to start.
- **Real-time website chat is your core need** → **LiveChat**. A polished, dedicated live chat platform with AI reply suggestions, visitor tracking, and transparent per-agent pricing from $19/month. Note ChatBot is a separate add-on.
- **You run a Shopify store** → **Gorgias**. Deep Shopify integration, ticket-based pricing from $10/month, and an AI Agent for order-status and returns. Better suited than Tidio to high order-management volume.
- **You need full ticketing, not just chat** → **Freshdesk**. Multi-channel ticketing, automations, SLAs, and a knowledge base with a free plan and paid tiers from $15/agent/month. The move when chat alone isn't enough.

## Bottom line

If AI is your priority, **Intercom** leads; if predictable pricing is, **Crisp** is the value pick. Choose **LiveChat** for dedicated real-time chat, **Gorgias** for Shopify, and **Freshdesk** when you've outgrown chat and need a real help desk. Run two in parallel for a week of live conversations before switching.
