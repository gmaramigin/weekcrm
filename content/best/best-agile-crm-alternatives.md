## Why look for an Agile CRM alternative

Agile CRM made its pitch on bundling: sales CRM, marketing automation, and helpdesk in one inexpensive package, targeted squarely at small businesses that didn't want to stitch together separate tools. For a while that was a compelling deal. But the product hasn't kept pace — the interface feels dated next to modern CRMs, users report inconsistent support, and the sense that development has slowed leaves teams worried about the roadmap. When you're trusting one platform with sales, marketing, and support, "is this still being actively built?" is a fair question, and for many it's the reason to move.

If you chose Agile CRM for the all-in-one value, the picks below deliver the same breadth on platforms that are clearly still investing.

## How we picked

We weighted four things: all-in-one breadth (sales, marketing, and service in one place), price and free-tier availability, how actively the product is developed and supported, and modern AI and automation. The list keeps the affordable, all-in-one spirit that drew people to Agile CRM.

## What to consider

- **You want the closest all-in-one match** → **EngageBay**. It mirrors Agile CRM's bundle — CRM, marketing automation, and helpdesk — at a similarly low price, but with more momentum behind the product and better support reputation.
- **You want the best long-term platform** → **HubSpot**. A free CRM to start, a clean modern interface, and the deepest ecosystem in the category. The Starter-to-Professional price jump is the trade-off, but the runway is unmatched.
- **You want value and deep customization** → **Zoho CRM**. Paired with Zoho's marketing and desk tools, it rebuilds Agile CRM's all-in-one promise with far more configurability and a strong price.
- **You want a free all-in-one with collaboration** → **Bitrix24**. Beyond CRM it adds tasks, chat, and a full workspace, free for unlimited users — the broadest free package if your team wants more than just sales tools.
- **You want a modern sales CRM with AI** → **Freshsales**. If the sales half of Agile CRM mattered most, Freshsales brings AI lead scoring, a built-in phone, and a clean interface that feels a generation newer.

## Bottom line

Stay on Agile CRM only if its price and feature mix still serve you and the roadmap concerns don't. Otherwise, switch to **EngageBay** for the closest all-in-one match, **HubSpot** for the strongest long-term platform, **Zoho CRM** for value, **Bitrix24** for a free workspace-plus-CRM, or **Freshsales** for modern sales. Migrate your marketing automations and pipelines together — keeping sales and marketing on one platform is the reason you chose an all-in-one in the first place.
