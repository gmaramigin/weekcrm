## Who should leave CompanyHub CRM

CompanyHub's pitch is real: custom fields, tables, and relationships you can shape without code, plus two-way email sync and solid reporting, from around $21/user/mo. For an SMB with a non-standard sales process that has outgrown a generic contact manager, that flexibility-per-dollar is genuinely attractive. The trouble is everything around the core. The integration marketplace is small, the mobile app and UI lag the mainstream tools, and product momentum from a small vendor is a bet you're making on your system of record. When you need a specific integration or a modern automation the platform doesn't have, there's often no marketplace app to fall back on.

You should leave if you're hitting integration walls, if you want a larger community and faster-moving product, or if the customization you loved now needs more automation and reporting muscle than CompanyHub provides. Stay if the low-cost, code-free customization is carrying your unusual process and you don't depend on a broad app ecosystem.

## What to consider

- **Best customization at scale** → Zoho CRM. The natural upgrade for a CompanyHub fan: the same "bend it to any process" flexibility but deeper — custom modules, workflows, Zia AI, and a huge marketplace — from ~$14/user/mo. You keep the adaptability and gain the ecosystem.
- **Best visual pipeline** → Pipedrive. If your process is really sales-first, Pipedrive delivers a fast, customizable pipeline with strong automation and a much larger integration catalog, without the config overhead.
- **Best for marketing-led growth** → HubSpot. A free CRM core plus best-in-class marketing tools when the gap you feel is email, content, and automation rather than raw field customization.
- **Best all-in-one flexibility** → Vtiger. One platform spanning sales, marketing, and support with heavy customization and a modern UI — the closest like-for-like to CompanyHub's "one flexible tool" promise, with more around it.
- **Best sales-automation value** → Salesmate. Customizable pipelines, built-in calling and sequences, and automation at an SMB-friendly price — strong when outreach volume is the priority.
- **Best for simplicity** → Capsule. If CompanyHub's flexibility has turned into fiddly maintenance, Capsule is a clean, fast CRM your team actually uses — contacts, pipeline, and tasks without the config burden.

## Match the alternative to the gap

Ask what CompanyHub's customization was buying you. If it was a genuinely bespoke data model, Zoho CRM and Vtiger let you keep that shape with a real ecosystem behind it. If it was just a pipeline that fit your stages, Pipedrive or Salesmate get you there faster with better integrations. If maintaining all that flexibility became the job, Capsule trades it for speed. And if marketing is now the constraint, HubSpot consolidates it.

## Trial advice

Custom-field-heavy CRMs are the hardest to migrate cleanly, so map your fields before you move. Export your contacts and deals with every custom field intact, load them into your top two picks, and rebuild your single most important automation in each. Run a live week and watch whether the new tool's marketplace covers the integrations CompanyHub couldn't — that's usually the real reason you're switching.
