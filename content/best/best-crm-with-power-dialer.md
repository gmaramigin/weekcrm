## How we picked

A power dialer only earns its keep when it's **native** to the CRM. The whole value is that every call, recording, and disposition logs itself against the right contact and deal — no copy-paste between a standalone dialer and your pipeline. We prioritized CRMs with calling built directly into the platform, ranked by dialer sophistication (power vs. predictive), call volume they're designed for, and how cleanly call activity flows into the rest of the workflow.

## What to consider

- **Dedicated high-volume outbound team** → Close. The benchmark: native power dialer (Professional) at up to 4x manual speed, predictive dialer (Business) that connects reps only on a human answer, plus call coaching. Built end-to-end for reps who live on the phone.
- **Value-focused team that also texts** → Salesmate. Native calling and SMS in one platform from $23/user/month, with AI call transcription and summaries.
- **Want AI on every call at a low price** → Freshsales. Freddy AI for scoring and insights, native calling, and a free plan to start — strong price-to-capability.
- **Messenger-first team adding voice** → Kommo. Best when your pipeline lives in WhatsApp and DMs but you still want calling in the mix; calling comes via integration rather than a native predictive dialer.
- **Pipeline CRM that needs calling** → Pipedrive. The cleanest visual pipeline with in-app calling and a deep Aircall integration when you outgrow the basics.

## Pricing snapshot

Calling tiers vary widely. Close's dialers live on its higher plans ($99 Professional for power, $129 Business for predictive). Salesmate starts at $23/user/month, Freshsales offers a free plan and paid tiers from $9/user/month, and Pipedrive starts at $14/user/month. Budget separately for telephony minutes and numbers — most vendors bill call usage on top of the seat price.

## Bottom line

If outbound calling is the core of your day, Close is the default — its native power and predictive dialers are the most mature in this list and built for exactly this. If you want native calling without Close's price, Salesmate and Freshsales both deliver. Pick by call volume: high-velocity teams need a true power or predictive dialer; lower-volume teams are fine with Pipedrive's in-app calling.
