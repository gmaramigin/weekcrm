## Why look for a Zoho Desk alternative

Zoho Desk is a strong, affordable help desk — context-aware ticketing, multi-channel queues, AI assistance, and a free tier for up to 3 agents. Its biggest advantage is native two-way sync with Zoho CRM. But that's also its catch: outside the Zoho ecosystem the value is less differentiated than Zendesk or Freshdesk. Advanced features like multi-department support, AI responses, and custom SLAs are gated to higher tiers, and the interface "can feel more complex than newer help desks." If you're not all-in on Zoho, it's worth comparing.

## How we picked

We weighted channel coverage, automation, time-to-value, and pricing transparency — plus fit for specific support models (ecommerce, SaaS, high-touch). Each pick is a credible standalone help desk that doesn't depend on a wider product suite to justify itself.

## What to consider

- **You want the closest all-round match** → **Freshdesk**. Multi-channel ticketing, powerful automations and SLAs, a self-service knowledge base, and a free plan, with paid tiers from $15/agent/month. The most natural Zoho Desk replacement.
- **You're scaling support fast** → **Zendesk**. The most mature, full-featured platform — ticketing, chat, voice, and help center in the Suite (from $55/agent/month), with 1,000+ integrations. Just watch total cost of ownership as add-ons accumulate.
- **You want support to feel human** → **Help Scout**. An inbox-style interface that feels like email, an AI assistant that resolves ~70% of routine requests, and the Beacon widget. Free for up to 5 users, paid from $25/user/month.
- **You run on Shopify** → **Gorgias**. Deep Shopify integration, ticket-based pricing from $10/month, and an AI Agent for routine order-status inquiries. The go-to for ecommerce brands drowning in "where's my order" tickets.
- **You're a SaaS product** → **Intercom**. AI-first support with the Fin agent (priced at $0.99 per resolved ticket) and best-in-class in-product messaging. Seats run $29–$132/month; model the usage-based AI costs before committing.

## Bottom line

For most teams leaving Zoho Desk, **Freshdesk** is the cleanest swap and **Zendesk** the enterprise-grade step up. Choose **Help Scout** for a warm, email-style experience, **Gorgias** for Shopify, and **Intercom** for AI-first SaaS support. Trial two with real tickets for a week before you migrate.
