## How we picked

Real-estate CRMs live or die on three things: **lead capture velocity** (web forms, IDX integration, portal leads), **follow-up automation** (drip nurture, SMS), and **transaction tracking** (pre-list, under contract, closed). Every pick below ships those three out of the box.

## What to consider

- **Solo agents** — pricing matters more than depth. Cloze and the entry tiers of Follow Up Boss are the sweet spot.
- **Teams** — go for round-robin lead routing and team accountability. BoldTrail and Lofty are built for this.
- **Brokerages** — Propertybase or BoldTrail's brokerage tier are the only ones that scale to 50+ seats without bolt-ons.

## Pricing snapshot

Real-estate CRMs cluster between $25 and $99/user/mo depending on tier. Lead-gen add-ons (IDX, Zillow integration) typically add another $50–$200/mo per seat, so the published price isn't the all-in.

## Trial advice

Don't pick a CRM cold — every vendor on this list offers a 14-day trial. Pick two, run them in parallel for one week of inbound leads, and keep whichever one your team actually checks every morning.
