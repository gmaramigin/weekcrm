## Why look for an Attio alternative

Attio is one of the best modern CRMs — AI-native, fast to deploy, and as flexible as a spreadsheet. But that flexibility cuts both ways. Teams outgrow it for a few predictable reasons: forecasting and full sales analytics are still limited, deep customization takes real trial and error, and the email experience shines most for Google Workspace users. Attio also stays deliberately lean — there's no native power dialer and no marketing suite — so phone-heavy or marketing-led teams end up bolting on extra tools.

If any of those is your bottleneck, the alternatives below each solve a specific part of it.

## How we picked

We weighted four things: fast time-to-value (Attio's biggest strength, so any replacement has to match it), depth where Attio is thin (forecasting, calling, marketing), pricing transparency, and adoption — a CRM only works if your team logs in without being told to. Every pick deploys in days, not months.

## What to consider

- **Relationship-led founders, agencies, VCs** → **folk**. Like Attio, it's contact-first and modern, but folkX captures LinkedIn profiles in one click and pricing starts at $20/user/month. It's the closest spiritual sibling to Attio for network-driven work.
- **You also need marketing** → **HubSpot**. Attio is sales-only by design; HubSpot bundles email marketing, forms, and a CMS, and its free tier is the most generous in the category. The trade-off is the price cliff from Starter ($20) to Professional ($100 + a $1,500 onboarding fee).
- **You want the simplest possible pipeline** → **Pipedrive**. A visual, drag-and-drop pipeline from $14/user/month with an AI Sales Assistant in every plan. Less customizable than Attio, but reps are productive on day one.
- **CRM adoption is your real problem** → **Salesflare**. It auto-fills itself from Gmail, Outlook, and LinkedIn — like Attio's enrichment, but tuned specifically for lean B2B sales teams under 50 people, from $29/user/month.
- **High-volume outbound** → **Close**. Native calling, SMS, and sequences out of the box ($19–$129/user/month). If your reps make 30+ calls a day, this is the one feature set Attio can't match.

## Bottom line

Stay on Attio if you love its modern, AI-native feel and your gaps are minor. Switch to **folk** if you're relationship-led, **HubSpot** if you need marketing, **Pipedrive** for a dead-simple pipeline, **Salesflare** if adoption keeps killing your CRM, or **Close** if you live on the phone. Run your top two in parallel for a week of real activity and keep whichever one your team actually opens.
