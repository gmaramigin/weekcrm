## Why look for a Teamgate alternative

Teamgate is a well-organized sales CRM: a clear pipeline, lead and customer management, scoring, and analytics that help small and mid-size sales teams stay on top of deals. For teams that want straightforward, sales-focused software without a steep learning curve, it does the job. But it's a more focused player than the category giants, which is where teams start to feel boundaries — fewer native integrations, less deep customization, and no built-in calling or marketing suite.

The teams that look around usually want one of a few things: a larger ecosystem and more configurability, native telephony for heavy outbound, marketing automation in the same platform, or a CRM that captures activity automatically so reps spend less time on data entry.

## What to consider

- **A simple, visual pipeline** → **Pipedrive**. The most widely adopted easy-to-use sales CRM, with drag-and-drop deal stages, automation, and an AI assistant from about $14/user/month.
- **Configurable depth and ecosystem** → **Zoho CRM**. Multi-pipeline management, process automation, Zia AI, and a vast app suite from a low per-seat price — the move when you want to tailor the CRM and connect it to everything.
- **High-volume outbound** → **Close**. Native dialer, SMS, and sequences out of the box make it the upgrade for teams whose day is dials and follow-ups.
- **Killing manual data entry** → **Salesflare**. It auto-captures contacts, emails, and meetings from your inbox and LinkedIn, keeping the pipeline current without anyone logging it.
- **Value plus marketing** → **Nutshell**. Unlimited contacts, native email marketing, and sequences from a low monthly price — strong for B2B teams that want CRM and outreach in one bill.

## Bottom line

Stay on Teamgate if its clean, sales-focused approach fits your team and nothing's holding you back. Otherwise, choose **Pipedrive** for the simplest pipeline, **Zoho CRM** for configurable depth and ecosystem, **Close** for outbound, **Salesflare** to end manual entry, or **Nutshell** for CRM plus marketing. Export your deals into your top two finalists and run a real week before committing.
