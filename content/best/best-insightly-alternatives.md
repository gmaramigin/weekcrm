## Why switch from Insightly

Insightly carved out a smart niche: an SMB CRM with native project management, so a service business can win a deal and then deliver on it without changing tools. For consultancies and professional-services firms, that sell-and-deliver loop is the appeal, and the free-for-two-users entry point lowers the barrier. The strain comes as you scale. Pricing climbs steeply from the $29/user/month Plus tier to $49 and $99 plans, the interface feels dated against newer competitors, and the project side handles tasks but lacks the time tracking, resource planning, and invoicing that a real delivery business eventually needs.

You should leave Insightly if the per-seat cost has jumped past what the product delivers, if the project module can't keep up with how you actually run engagements, or if you want a more modern, more configurable platform. Teams that should stay are small service shops whose projects really are just task lists attached to deals, and who are still inside the lower tiers.

## What to look for in an Insightly alternative

- **Best all-in-one for service firms** &rarr; Teamleader. CRM, project management, *and* invoicing in one product built for agencies and consultants, from €37/month. The natural swap when you want the whole sell-deliver-bill cycle in one place.
- **Best for breadth** &rarr; Vtiger CRM. Combines sales, marketing, and help desk in a single platform from $12/user/month, with a free open-source edition. The move for teams that want Insightly-style all-in-one without the price jumps.
- **Best for sales depth plus projects** &rarr; Zoho CRM. Deeper pipeline management, automation, and Zia AI from $14–$52/user/month, free for three users — and Zoho Projects plugs into the same ecosystem. The pick when the CRM side needs to grow up.
- **Best for true PSA** &rarr; Scoro. Full professional-services automation — projects, resourcing, time tracking, and invoicing — from $19.90/user/month. The upgrade for agencies whose delivery is too complex for task lists.
- **Best feature-dense free option** &rarr; Bitrix24. CRM, project management, and team collaboration in one platform with a free plan and paid tiers from a flat $49/month for unlimited users. The move when budget is tight but you still want CRM plus delivery.

## Pricing snapshot

Insightly runs free for two users, then $29, $49, and $99/user/month — and the useful tiers sit at the top of that range. The alternatives are mostly cheaper or more flexible: Vtiger from $12 (free open-source edition), Zoho CRM from $14 (free for three users), Scoro from $19.90, Teamleader from €37/month, and Bitrix24 with a free plan plus a flat $49/month for unlimited users. The flat-rate options (Bitrix24, and Teamleader's bundled model) get especially attractive as headcount grows, since you're not paying per seat for the delivery side. Match the tool to where Insightly broke for you — if it was the projects, go Scoro or Teamleader; if it was the CRM, go Zoho or Vtiger — then run your real workflow through it before renewal.
