## Who should leave Trengo

Trengo's strength is the unified omnichannel inbox: WhatsApp, live chat, email, social, and voice flow into one shared workspace, with team collaboration features that make it easy for a group to handle conversations together. For businesses where WhatsApp and messaging are the primary support channels — common in Europe and in retail, travel, and services — Trengo fits the way customers actually reach out.

Where it starts to strain is depth. Trengo is more shared inbox than full help desk, so teams that need formal ticket numbers, multi-tier SLAs, sophisticated automation, or rich agent analytics can outgrow it. Its AI and flow-builder cover common cases but don't match the autonomous resolution of the leading AI-first platforms. As volume and complexity rise, the same simplicity that made Trengo easy to adopt can become the reason to move on.

## What to consider

- **Best for AI-first omnichannel** → Intercom. The Fin AI Agent resolves a large share of conversations autonomously across chat and messaging, and you pay per resolved issue rather than per attempt. Strong for product-led teams that want AI deeply tied to the in-product experience, with WhatsApp and social in the same inbox.
- **Best for ticketing depth** → Freshdesk. Adds a real ticketing backbone under your chat channels: multi-channel ticketing, multi-tier SLAs, automation workflows, and reporting, with a free plan for unlimited agents on email. The pick when you want structure beneath the conversations.
- **Best for scaling omnichannel** → Zendesk. Mature triggers, macros, SLA routing, and a 1,000+ app marketplace, with broad channel coverage. The safe choice when support is growing fast and you want a platform that won't hit a ceiling.
- **Best affordable omnichannel inbox** → Crisp. The closest in spirit to Trengo — chat, email, and social in one shared inbox with a chatbot and a low entry price. A good fit for small teams that want omnichannel without enterprise pricing.
- **Best for cross-team conversations** → Front. When WhatsApp and chat threads need to pass through sales, logistics, or finance before they close, Front's routing, assignment, and SLA tools handle the handoff cleanly across every channel.

## Choosing your next tool

Start with what's pushing you off Trengo. If it's AI — you want conversations resolved automatically, not just organized — Intercom is the clearest upgrade. If it's structure — you need ticket numbers, SLAs, and reporting under your channels — Freshdesk or Zendesk give you a real help desk, with Freshdesk winning on price and Zendesk on ecosystem depth.

If you actually like Trengo's lightweight omnichannel approach and just want a different (or cheaper) take on it, Crisp is the closest swap. And if your real problem is that messaging threads keep bouncing between departments, Front is built for exactly that. Run a live week in two finalists with your real WhatsApp and chat volume before deciding.
