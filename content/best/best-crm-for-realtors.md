## How we picked

Real estate runs on speed-to-lead and consistent follow-up, so we judged these CRMs on how fast a new inquiry gets into an agent's hands and how relentlessly the system keeps nurturing it. We looked for **automated drip and SMS campaigns**, **mobile apps agents actually use in the field**, **transaction and pipeline tracking through close**, and **portal integrations** (Zillow, Realtor.com) that drop leads straight into routing. Tools that demanded heavy setup before a single text could go out were marked down — agents live in the car, not at a desk.

## What to consider

- **Solo agent, modest lead budget** → **Wise Agent**. Flat price, no per-seat math, full transaction management.
- **Lead-gen-first, want the website included** → **BoldTrail** or **Lofty**. Both bundle IDX and ad-driven lead capture, so the CRM and the funnel are one system.
- **Team that buys leads and needs accountability** → **Follow Up Boss**. Its routing and activity tracking expose which agents are actually working their pipeline.
- **Farming a geographic area or sphere** → **Top Producer**, built around drip campaigns and neighborhood farming.
- **Listing-heavy seller agents** → favor tools (Lofty) that surface seller-intent signals inside your existing database.

## Pricing snapshot

The split is between marketing-CRM tools and all-in-one platforms. **Wise Agent** ($49/mo flat), **Follow Up Boss** (from $69/user/mo), and **Top Producer** (from $179/user/mo) sell CRM and marketing. **Lofty** (~$449/mo) and **BoldTrail** (~$499/mo) bundle an IDX website and lead generation, so compare total cost of ownership, not sticker price — a cheaper CRM plus portal leads can cost more than an all-in-one.

## IDX, lead routing, and speed-to-lead

The number that predicts conversion in this category is response time. **Follow Up Boss** wins on routing mechanics: the first agent to claim a lead gets it, and managers see who's slow. **BoldTrail** and **Lofty** close the loop differently — because the IDX site, the lead, and the CRM are the same platform, a portal visitor's property searches feed straight into automated follow-up. If you already buy Zillow or Realtor.com leads, prioritize routing speed; if you want to own your funnel end to end, buy the platform that includes the website.
