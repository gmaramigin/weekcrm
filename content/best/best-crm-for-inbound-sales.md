## How we picked

Inbound sales is a speed-and-sorting problem. Leads arrive on their own schedule, in volume, and the team that contacts them first and prioritizes them best wins. We ranked CRMs on three things: how cleanly they capture a lead at the source (forms, chat, API), how fast and how intelligently they route and score it, and how immediately follow-up fires. A CRM that requires a rep to manually claim, qualify, and sequence every form fill loses the race before the rep even opens it. Enrichment counted too — an inbound lead that arrives with company size and role already filled saves the qualifying call.

## What to consider

- **End-to-end inbound funnel in one tool** → HubSpot. Forms, chatbots, scoring, routing, and sequences share one record, so leads never fall through a handoff gap.
- **Auto-enriching inbound leads** → Attio. New contacts get company and role data filled on arrival, so reps qualify from a complete picture instead of a name and email.
- **Budget-conscious teams that still want automation** → Zoho CRM. Web-to-lead forms, assignment rules, and workflow triggers on an inexpensive plan.
- **AI scoring without enterprise pricing** → Freshsales. Freddy's contact scoring surfaces the hottest leads at a fraction of HubSpot Pro's cost.
- **Small team that just needs fast follow-up** → Pipedrive. A clean pipeline plus simple automations to ensure no new lead sits untouched.

## Pricing snapshot

Zoho CRM is free for three users and about $14/user/month on Standard. Pipedrive starts at $14/user/month. Freshsales runs free to roughly $59/user/month, with AI scoring appearing on its mid tier. HubSpot is free to start but reaches $90+/user/month on Sales Pro where predictive scoring and advanced routing unlock. Attio sits at $29–$119/seat, with automatic enrichment usable from the middle plan upward.

## Trial advice

Run a live lead through the whole machine. Submit your own web form, then time how long until it's scored, routed to the right rep, and hit with the first auto-response — that interval is the metric that predicts your conversion rate. Check whether the lead arrives enriched or as a bare email, and test what happens to leads that come in after hours. The CRM that gets a stranger from form fill to a sequenced, prioritized record with zero clicks from your team is the one that will actually move your inbound numbers.
