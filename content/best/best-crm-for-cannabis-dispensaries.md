## How we picked

Cannabis retail is two businesses at once: a high-volume consumer business that lives on loyalty and retention, and (for many operators) a B2B wholesale operation moving product to other licensees. A general CRM serves the customer-relationship and marketing layer — it does **not** replace a compliant seed-to-sale POS. We prioritized loyalty and retention marketing, multi-location support, B2B pipeline strength, and value, while flagging the compliance constraints that make cannabis marketing different from any other vertical.

## What to consider

- **Retention marketing and a free start** → HubSpot. The strongest email and marketing automation in this list, with a genuinely useful free CRM to begin.
- **Maximum value with real depth** → Zoho CRM. Multi-pipeline management and Zia AI at $14–$52/user/month, free for up to 3 users — hard to beat on price-to-capability.
- **B2B wholesale pipeline** → Pipedrive. The cleanest way to track wholesale buyers and orders through stages, at $14/user/month.
- **Multi-location, per-organization pricing** → Bitrix24. Paid plans from $49/month flat for unlimited users, plus collaboration and telephony — efficient for a chain with many staff.
- **Large multi-state operator** → Salesforce. Custom objects, enterprise reporting, and the deepest ecosystem — worth its weight only at real scale with a dedicated admin.

## A note on compliance

This is non-negotiable in cannabis. These CRMs are the marketing and relationship layer, not your compliance system — keep inventory and sales in a Metrc-integrated POS. Cannabis SMS and email marketing is restricted by state regulation and by carrier and platform policy; some providers prohibit cannabis messaging outright. Confirm consent, opt-out handling, and platform eligibility before you run a single campaign.

## Pricing snapshot

HubSpot and Zoho both have free tiers; Zoho paid runs $14–$52/user/month, HubSpot paid from $20/user/month (with a steep jump to $100 at Professional). Pipedrive starts at $14/user/month. Bitrix24's per-organization model (from $49/month flat) is the value play for large headcounts. Salesforce starts at $25/user/month and climbs fast once add-ons and implementation are counted.

## Bottom line

For most single-store or small-chain dispensaries, start with HubSpot (for retention marketing) or Zoho CRM (for value and automation). Wholesale-led operators should test Pipedrive's pipeline. Reserve Salesforce for multi-state scale. Whatever you choose, treat it as the layer on top of your compliant POS — and lock down your marketing compliance before turning on outreach.
