## How we picked

"Call tracking" here means the CRM itself handles the phone — a native dialer, automatic logging of every call to the right record, recording, and ideally local-presence numbers — rather than relying on a separate telephony product. We prioritized depth of built-in calling: click-to-dial from any record, automatic call logging with notes and recordings attached, power or predictive dialing for outbound volume, and call analytics that roll into pipeline reporting. We also valued local-number provisioning to lift connect rates. CRMs that only integrate a third-party phone (rather than offering a native dialer) scored lower, because that's exactly the disconnected setup these picks are meant to replace.

## What to consider

- **High-volume outbound dialing** → Close. Built-in power and predictive dialers keep reps on live conversations.
- **Best value virtual phone system** → Salesmate. A full built-in phone with calling, texting, and power dialing at SMB pricing.
- **Calling plus AI lead scoring** → Freshsales. The Freshcaller dialer pairs with Freddy AI to prioritize who to call next.
- **Cheapest built-in telephony** → Bitrix24. Native call-center features available even on the free plan.
- **Calls mixed with messaging** → Kommo. Logs calls alongside WhatsApp, Instagram, and chat in one conversational pipeline.

## Pricing snapshot

CRM subscriptions here run from free (Bitrix24, Freshsales entry tier) to $49/mo (Close), with Salesmate from $23/seat, Freshsales paid from $9/seat, and Kommo from $15/seat. Critically, calling minutes and phone numbers are usually billed separately as telephony credits on top of the seat price — so the real monthly cost depends on call volume. Always model expected minutes, not just the per-seat fee, when comparing these tools.

## Dialer, recording, and local-presence depth

The features that actually move connect and conversion rates are the ones buried in the telephony layer. A power dialer auto-advances through a list so reps don't dial by hand; a predictive dialer (Close's strength) goes further, calling ahead of rep availability for the highest-volume teams. Call recording with searchable transcripts turns conversations into coachable, auditable records attached to each deal — standard in Close, Salesmate, and Freshsales. Local-presence numbers, which show a recipient a familiar area code, can meaningfully raise answer rates for outbound teams; confirm availability and per-number cost in your target regions. Finally, check that call outcomes and durations flow into pipeline reporting, so calling activity is measured against revenue rather than sitting in a separate phone dashboard.
